Boxing in Copenhagen is making headlines, and Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC) is at the heart of the excitement. As recently highlighted by TV2 Nyheder, AOBC stands out as a vibrant hub for fitness, skill-building, and community in Hellerup and Charlottenlund. Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, this premier boxing gym is where newcomers and seasoned fighters alike step up to challenge themselves, get fit, and grow—both inside and outside the ring.

#### Why AOBC Is Copenhagen’s Go-To Boxing Gym

Ashira Oure Boxing Club isn’t just another boxing gym in Copenhagen. It’s a welcoming space where everyone, from complete beginners to competitive athletes, is encouraged to achieve their best. Here’s what makes AOBC unique:

- **Expert Leadership:** Evans Ashira brings championship experience and a passion for nurturing talent. Supported by coaches like Yuna Ashira, Lucas Ashira, and Mino Ashira, every session is anchored in expertise and motivation.
- **Inclusive Programs for All Ages:** Whether you’re searching for beginner boxing classes, youth boxing, or boxing training for kids, AOBC offers tailored classes that focus on skill, fitness, and discipline.
- **Diverse Training Opportunities:** From sparring classes and competition prep to fitness boxing and personal coaching, there’s something for every goal and fitness level.
- **Community Spirit:** AOBC is more than a gym—it’s a supportive boxing community where each member finds encouragement, camaraderie, and inspiration.

#### Boxing for Beginners and Beyond

Starting your boxing journey can feel intimidating, but at AOBC, the atmosphere is supportive and empowering. The club’s beginner boxing classes and boxing club for beginners are designed to help you build confidence, master the basics, and enjoy the process. Coaches guide you through every punch, helping you create a solid foundation—whether your focus is fitness, technique, or competition.

For youth and families, AOBC’s youth boxing and kids boxing near me programs are a perfect introduction to boxing, blending fun with essential skills and discipline. Safety is a top priority, and each session incorporates boxing safety tips and structured drills.

#### Fitness and Discipline: The AOBC Advantage

Ashira Oure Boxing Club believes that boxing is for everyone. The blend of boxing and fitness classes, sparring opportunities, and advanced competition training means that every member, regardless of experience, can find their path. Members benefit from:

- **Personalized coaching and training plans**
- **Access to top-tier facilities in Hellerup and Charlottenlund**
- **A clear boxing training schedule tailored to individual goals**
- **Guidance on how to train for boxing, boxing workout plans, and sparring rules**

Whether you aspire to compete or simply want to improve your fitness, AOBC’s holistic approach ensures you reach your potential.

With the recent spotlight from TV2 Nyheder, there’s never been a better time to discover the thriving scene at Ashira Oure Boxing Club. AOBC is proud to be a leading copenhagen boxing club, dedicated to health, wellness, and personal growth. Step into a gym with boxing classes that inspire, challenge, and transform.
