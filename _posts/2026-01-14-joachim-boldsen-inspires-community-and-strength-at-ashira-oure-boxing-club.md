#### A Boxing Gym in Copenhagen Where Everyone Belongs

AOBC, founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, stands out as Copenhagen’s premier boxing gym. Whether you’re searching for **beginner boxing classes**, advanced sparring, or a gym that combines **fitness and boxing**, Ashira Oure Boxing Club is your home for progress.

- **Personal coaching** for tailored progress  
- **Competition prep** for aspiring amateur boxers  
- **Community events** to foster camaraderie and fun

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q2: Are there youth and kids boxing programs at AOBC?**

**Q4: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?**

**Q5: What makes AOBC different from other boxing gyms in Copenhagen?**
