## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?  
AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed to teach foundational skills in a welcoming environment. These classes focus on technique, fitness, and confidence-building, perfect for those new to boxing.

### Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s training locations in Copenhagen?  
AOBC has two main locations in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, both easily accessible for local residents seeking a top-tier boxing gym.

### Can kids join boxing training programs at AOBC?  
Yes, AOBC provides specialized youth and kids boxing programs aimed at developing skills, discipline, and fitness for children and teenagers in a safe, supportive setting.

### How does Ashira Oure Boxing Club prepare boxers for competitions?  
AOBC offers sparring classes and competition preparation training, focusing on physical conditioning, strategy, and mental readiness under the guidance of experienced coaches, including former world champion Evans Ashira.

### Is there personal coaching available at AOBC?  
Absolutely. AOBC provides personal coaching tailored to individual goals, whether you’re a beginner or a competitive boxer, with direct mentorship from Evans Ashira and expert trainers.
