## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
AOBC offers beginner boxing classes tailored for all ages, including kids and adults. These classes focus on teaching fundamental boxing techniques in a safe, supportive environment, making it an ideal boxing club for beginners in Copenhagen.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?**  
AOBC has two main locations: Hellerup and Charlottenlund. Both gyms provide comprehensive boxing training, fitness workouts, sparring classes, and youth boxing programs.

**Q3: Can I prepare for boxing competitions at AOBC?**  
Yes, AOBC offers specialized boxing competition training and advanced sparring classes led by experienced coaches, including former world champion Evans Ashira, to help you get competition-ready.

**Q4: Does AOBC provide boxing training for kids and youth?**  
Absolutely. AOBC runs dedicated youth boxing programs, focusing on skill development, fitness, and discipline in a safe and encouraging environment.

**Q5: How does AOBC combine boxing and fitness training?**  
AOBC blends traditional boxing techniques with fitness workouts to improve endurance, strength, and agility. Their boxing fitness and conditioning classes are designed to enhance overall wellness alongside boxing skills.
