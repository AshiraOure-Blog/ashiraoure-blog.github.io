## Experience Top-Tier Boxing in Copenhagen

Whether you’re searching for a boxing club in Copenhagen to kickstart your fitness journey or aiming to sharpen your skills for competition, AOBC stands as the city’s premier destination for boxing excellence. Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, our club is built on a legacy of achievement and a commitment to welcoming everyone—beginners, fitness enthusiasts, youth, and competitive boxers alike.

### Why Choose Ashira Oure Boxing Club?

- **Expert-Led Classes:** Learn from experienced coaches, including Evans Ashira, who bring world-class techniques and real-world insights to every training session.
- **All Levels Welcome:** From your first boxing class to advanced sparring and competition training, AOBC meets you at your level and supports your growth.
- **Community First:** Our members form a tight-knit, supportive community, united by a shared passion for boxing and fitness.
- **Holistic Approach:** We believe in developing not only physical strength but also discipline, resilience, and confidence.
- **Youth and Family Focus:** With youth boxing and kids boxing programs, AOBC is a safe, inspiring space for young athletes and families in Hellerup, Charlottenlund, and across Copenhagen.

## Boxing Classes That Match Your Ambition

At AOBC, you’ll find a wide range of classes and programs designed to help you reach your goals:

- **Beginner Boxing Classes:** Perfect for those new to the sport, focusing on fundamentals, fitness, and fun.
- **Sparring and Competition Prep:** Take your skills to the next level with structured sparring classes and comprehensive competition training.
- **Personal Coaching:** One-on-one sessions tailored to your needs, whether you’re training for your first bout or refining your technique.
- **Fitness Boxing:** High-energy workouts that blend boxing drills with conditioning for a complete fitness experience.
- **Youth Programs:** Safe, engaging classes for kids and teenagers, building confidence and healthy habits from an early age.

Ashira Oure Boxing Club stands out due to its expert-led classes by world champion Evans Ashira, a welcoming environment for all skill levels, comprehensive boxing programs including sparring and competition training, and a strong community focus in locations such as Hellerup and Charlottenlund.

### Do you offer beginner boxing classes for those new to the sport?

Yes, AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed to teach the fundamentals of boxing, improve fitness, and ensure a fun and supportive experience for newcomers to boxing in Copenhagen.

### Are there boxing programs available for kids and youth?

Absolutely. AOBC provides safe and engaging youth boxing programs focused on building confidence, discipline, and healthy habits for kids and teenagers in the Copenhagen area.

### Can I get personal coaching at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?

Yes, AOBC offers personalized one-on-one coaching tailored to your goals, whether you’re preparing for your first boxing match or looking to improve specific techniques.
