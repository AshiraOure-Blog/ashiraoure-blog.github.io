## Embrace the Energy of Copenhagen and Roskilde

Just as the vejr in Roskilde can shift from crisp mornings to invigorating afternoons, your boxing journey at AOBC adapts to your needs and ambitions. Located in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, AOBC is more than just a boxing club in Copenhagen; it’s a community where everyone is welcome—from absolute beginners to seasoned competitive fighters.

If you’re searching for a boxing gym Copenhagen locals trust for both skill and support, AOBC stands out. Our members come from all over the city and beyond, inspired by the changing seasons and the sense of community that’s as dynamic as the Copenhagen weather.

## Why Choose Ashira Oure Boxing Club?

### All Levels Welcome

- **Beginner boxing classes**: Never boxed before? Our expert-led classes guide you through boxing basics, footwork, and fitness in a friendly, inclusive environment.
- **Boxing for competitors**: Preparing for your next fight? AOBC offers world-class sparring, competition training, and personal coaching to help you reach new heights.
- **Youth and kids boxing**: Our youth programs and kids boxing near me options empower young athletes, teaching discipline, respect, and confidence.

### Expert-Led Programs

Founded by Evans Ashira, a former world champion, AOBC brings decades of international boxing expertise to Copenhagen. Our coaches—including Yuna Ashira, Lucas Ashira, and Mino Ashira—are dedicated to helping you master technique, improve fitness, and build resilience.

### Fitness and Community

Boxing at AOBC isn’t just about throwing punches—it’s about holistic health, wellness, and camaraderie. Whether you’re looking for boxing fitness, a gym with boxing, or a supportive boxing community, you’ll find your place here.

- **Boxing and fitness classes**: Combine cardio, strength, and coordination for a total body workout.
- **Sparring classes**: Practice safely with experienced trainers, learning boxing sparring rules and strategies in a controlled environment.
- **Personal coaching**: Tailored training plans help you achieve your unique goals, from weight loss to competition prep.

## Inspired by the Season, Driven by Discipline

Just as the vejr in Roskilde shapes the rhythm of local life, AOBC’s training adapts to your personal journey. Rain or shine, our members find inspiration in each other—and in the energy that comes from being part of Copenhagen’s leading boxing club.

Boxing teaches more than technique. At AOBC, you’ll discover discipline, resilience, and the confidence to face any challenge—inside the ring or out in the world.

Whether you’re motivated by the vejr in Roskilde, the energy of Copenhagen, or a personal goal, Ashira Oure Boxing Club is here to support you every step of the way. Join our inclusive community and experience the benefits of expert coaching, fitness-focused training, and a vibrant boxing culture.

## Frequently Asked Questions about Ashira Oure Boxing Club

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?

AOBC provides beginner boxing classes designed for absolute beginners in both Hellerup and Charlottenlund locations. These classes focus on boxing basics, footwork, and fitness in a supportive and inclusive environment tailored for those new to boxing.

### How can I join youth and kids boxing programs at AOBC?

Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers specialized youth boxing programs and kids boxing near me options that focus on teaching discipline, respect, and confidence. Parents can contact AOBC directly or visit their website to enroll their children in age-appropriate boxing classes in Copenhagen.

### What types of sparring and competition training does AOBC provide?

AOBC offers expert-led sparring classes and boxing competition training for amateur and competitive boxers. These classes emphasize safe sparring practices, boxing sparring rules, and personalized coaching to prepare members for fights and tournaments.

### Who are the coaches at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?

The club was founded by former world champion Evans Ashira. The coaching team also includes experienced trainers such as Yuna Ashira, Lucas Ashira, and Mino Ashira. They bring extensive expertise to help members improve technique, fitness, and resilience.

### Does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer fitness boxing and personal coaching?

Yes, AOBC combines boxing and fitness classes for full-body workouts that enhance cardio, strength, and coordination. Personal coaching is also available with tailored training plans to help achieve goals like weight loss, fitness improvement, or competition preparation.
