When TV2 Sport shines a spotlight on athletic achievement and community, it’s clear why Copenhagen’s own Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC) stands out. In the heart of Hellerup and Charlottenlund, AOBC is more than a boxing gym—it’s where champions are made and friendships are forged. Whether you’re a newcomer searching for “boxing club for beginners” or a seasoned competitor seeking the next challenge, AOBC welcomes you.

#### Experience Boxing Excellence with Evans Ashira

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC is a boxing club Copenhagen athletes trust for quality, inclusivity, and results. Evans’ vision is simple yet powerful: create a space where anyone—regardless of age or experience—can build skill, fitness, and discipline. This commitment is why AOBC has become a beacon for those looking for the best boxing gym Copenhagen offers.

**What sets AOBC apart?**

- **Expert-led Classes:** Every session is guided by seasoned trainers, including Evans Ashira himself. You’ll learn proven boxing drills, techniques, and safety tips essential for every level.
- **Supportive Community:** AOBC is more than a gym with boxing—it’s a family. From your first boxing class to your hundredth sparring session, you’ll be surrounded by encouragement and camaraderie.
- **Diverse Programs:** Try everything from fitness boxing and sparring classes to youth boxing and personal coaching. AOBC also offers specialized programs for kids, making it the go-to for “kids boxing near me” in Copenhagen.

#### Boxing for Every Level

TV2 Sport’s coverage reminds us that athletic journeys are unique. At AOBC, members range from those stepping into the ring for the first time to competitive fighters prepping for their next big match. Beginners are guided through the basics, including boxing safety tips and foundational boxing workout plans. Experienced athletes benefit from advanced sparring classes, competition training, and tailored coaching to sharpen their edge.

- **Beginner Boxing Classes:** Perfect for those new to the sport, focusing on technique, fitness, and confidence-building.
- **Youth Boxing and Kids Programs:** AOBC is proud to nurture the next generation with age-appropriate training, discipline, and fun.
- **Competition Prep:** For those aiming higher, AOBC offers intensive training schedules, boxing sparring rules, and expert mentorship to help you compete at your best.

#### Building Fitness and Character

Fitness at AOBC goes beyond physical strength. Members discover resilience, mental toughness, and a sense of belonging. The club’s approach fuses traditional boxing with modern wellness, inspired by the Ashira family’s commitment to health. Programs like Ashira Wellness and Oure Fitness complement the core boxing academy, supporting holistic growth.

#### Join the AOBC Community

Inspired by TV2 Sport’s celebration of athletic spirit, AOBC continues to champion every member’s journey. Whether you’re searching for a Copenhagen boxing club to kickstart your fitness, a place for your child to learn discipline, or a hub for amateur boxing club competition, AOBC is ready to welcome you.

Ready to step into the ring with Copenhagen’s leading boxing community? [Discover classes and membership options at Ashira Oure Boxing Club](https://www.ashiraoure.com/) and become part of something extraordinary. 

Ashira Oure Boxing Club—where every punch builds strength, every class creates unity, and every member is a champion in the making.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners?**  
A1: AOBC offers beginner boxing classes focusing on technique, fitness, and confidence-building. These classes are designed to introduce newcomers to the fundamentals of boxing in a supportive environment.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?**  
A2: Ashira Oure Boxing Club has two locations in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, making it convenient for members across the region.

**Q3: Does AOBC provide youth and kids boxing programs?**  
A3: Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth and kids boxing programs that focus on age-appropriate training, discipline, and fun, catering to young boxing enthusiasts in Copenhagen.

**Q4: Can I join sparring and competition training at AOBC?**  
A4: Absolutely. AOBC provides sparring classes and competition preparation with expert coaching, ideal for amateur boxers and those aiming to compete professionally.

**Q5: How does Ashira Oure Boxing Club integrate fitness and wellness into its boxing training?**  
A5: Along with traditional boxing training, AOBC incorporates programs like Ashira Wellness and Oure Fitness to promote holistic health, mental toughness, and overall fitness.
