Whether you are looking for your first taste of boxing or want to refine your skills for competition, AOBC offers a diverse “menu” that caters to all levels:

- **Beginner boxing classes**: Perfect for those stepping into the ring for the first time, our classes break down the basics in a friendly, encouraging environment.
- **Boxing and fitness workouts**: Blend the intensity of boxing with full-body fitness for a workout that challenges both body and mind.
- **Sparring and competition prep**: For aspiring competitors, our sparring sessions follow safe, structured **boxing sparring rules** and our expert coaches help you prepare for amateur or professional bouts.
- **Youth boxing programs**: AOBC is a top **amateur boxing club** for young athletes. Our youth and kids’ programs focus on discipline, self-esteem, and fun—making us the answer to “kids boxing near me” in Copenhagen.
- **Personal coaching**: Train one-on-one with our world-class coaching team, including Evans Ashira himself, and develop a tailored **boxing workout plan** to meet your goals.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners?**  
A1: Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers beginner boxing classes designed to teach the fundamentals in a supportive environment. These classes focus on basic techniques, boxing drills, and fitness to help newcomers build confidence and skill safely.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?**  
A2: AOBC has two convenient locations in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund. Both gyms provide state-of-the-art facilities and expert coaching for all levels.

**Q3: Does AOBC offer youth and kids boxing programs?**  
A3: Yes, AOBC provides specialized youth boxing programs that focus on discipline, fitness, and fun. These classes are ideal for children and teens looking for a positive, structured boxing environment near Copenhagen.

**Q4: Can I get personal coaching or competition training at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**  
A4: Absolutely. AOBC offers one-on-one personal coaching and tailored boxing competition training, led by world champion Evans Ashira and his experienced coaching team.

**Q5: How does Ashira Oure Boxing Club prioritize safety during sparring sessions?**  
A5: AOBC follows strict boxing sparring rules and safety protocols to ensure all sparring classes are conducted in a secure, controlled environment. Coaches supervise sessions closely to maintain safety and promote skill development.
