In Copenhagen, **grundlovsdag** is a day when the city’s energy feels especially vibrant—a time to celebrate freedom, community, and personal growth. At **Ashira Oure Boxing Club** (AOBC), these values are the core of everything we do. Founded by former world champion **Evans Ashira**, AOBC is more than just a boxing gym. We are a thriving, supportive community where beginners, fitness enthusiasts, youth, and competitive boxers all come together to build strength, discipline, and confidence.

#### Why Grundlovsdag Matters at AOBC in Copenhagen

Grundlovsdag is about honoring the foundations of Danish society. In many ways, the journey in the boxing ring mirrors the journey toward personal freedom and self-mastery. Whether you’re stepping into the gym for your first **beginner boxing class** or preparing for your next match, every punch thrown and every round completed at AOBC is a step toward becoming your best self in **boxing Copenhagen**.

#### Boxing for Every Level and Every Ambition at AOBC

At **Ashira Oure Boxing Club**, we welcome everyone—from total beginners to seasoned athletes. Our expert coaches, led by **Evans Ashira** himself, bring world-class experience to every session. Here’s how we help our members grow:

- **Beginner Boxing Classes** in Copenhagen: Perfect for those taking their first step into the world of boxing, our classes focus on basic techniques, fitness, and safety. You don’t need any prior experience—just a desire to learn and improve.
- **Youth and Kids Boxing Programs**: Our youth programs inspire confidence, discipline, and healthy habits. Kids can discover the thrill of the sport in a safe, structured setting with experienced coaches. If you’re searching for **kids boxing near me** or **boxing training for kids** in Copenhagen, AOBC is your go-to.
- **Sparring and Competition Training**: For those ready to test their skills, we offer sparring classes and dedicated competition prep. Learn about **boxing sparring rules**, develop your strategy, and push your limits with the support of our community.
- **Personal Coaching**: Whether you’re training for fitness, competition, or personal growth, our one-on-one coaching helps you achieve your unique goals.

#### Building a Thriving Boxing Community in Copenhagen and Hellerup

AOBC is not just a **boxing club Copenhagen**—it’s a place where friendships are forged, goals are set, and achievements are celebrated. From our gyms in **Hellerup boxing gym** and Charlottenlund, we support all members, regardless of age or experience. Our focus on **boxing fitness**, discipline, and mutual respect creates a welcoming atmosphere for everyone.

We know that joining a new gym or trying a new sport can be intimidating. That’s why our trainers and members work together to foster an inclusive environment where everyone is encouraged to challenge themselves and grow.

#### Celebrate Your Strength This Grundlovsdag at Ashira Oure Boxing Club

On grundlovsdag, as Copenhagen comes together to celebrate freedom and community, we invite you to experience the unique spirit of **Ashira Oure Boxing Club**. Whether you want to boost your fitness, learn the fundamentals of boxing, or compete at the highest level, AOBC is here to guide you every step of the way.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed for those new to the sport. These classes focus on basic boxing techniques, fitness, and safety, making them perfect for anyone wanting to start boxing in Copenhagen.

**Q2: Does AOBC have boxing programs for kids and youth?**  
A2: Yes, AOBC provides structured youth and kids boxing programs that promote confidence, discipline, and healthy habits in a safe environment. These programs are ideal for parents searching for "kids boxing near me" in Copenhagen.

**Q3: Can I train for boxing competitions at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**  
A3: Absolutely. AOBC offers sparring classes and dedicated competition training to help amateur and competitive boxers prepare effectively, including learning boxing sparring rules and developing fight strategies.

**Q4: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?**  
A4: AOBC has two main gym locations in Copenhagen: Hellerup and Charlottenlund. Both facilities provide comprehensive boxing training and fitness programs to suit all levels.

**Q5: Who founded Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**  
A5: The club was founded by former world champion **Evans Ashira**, who brings extensive experience and passion to the coaching team at AOBC.
