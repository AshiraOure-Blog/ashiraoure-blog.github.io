- **Beginners eager to try their first boxing class**  
- **Fitness enthusiasts looking for a dynamic workout**  
- **Youth pursuing confidence and discipline**  
- **Amateur and competitive fighters determined to go further**

Whether you join us at our Hellerup boxing gym or Charlottenlund location, Ashira Oure Boxing Club is more than a gym—it’s a vibrant boxing community. Our expert trainers lead **boxing classes**, **boxing and fitness classes**, **sparring sessions**, and **personal coaching** tailored to your goals, just like a coach guides a promising athlete to the top.

## From Beginner to Champion—Everyone Belongs at AOBC

Just as Alexander Sørloth honed his craft over years of dedication, AOBC members work with skilled coaches to master boxing fundamentals, fitness, and strategy. If you’re new to the sport, our **beginner boxing classes** and **boxing club for beginners** provide a welcoming space to learn boxing’s essentials at your own pace.

For youth and kids, our **youth boxing** and **boxing training for kids** programs build confidence, coordination, and discipline in a supportive setting. Our philosophy: every aspiring athlete deserves expert attention and a clear path to growth.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
AOBC provides beginner boxing classes designed for newcomers to learn boxing fundamentals, build fitness, and develop confidence in a supportive environment. Classes are available at both Hellerup and Charlottenlund locations.

**Q2: Can kids and youth join boxing training programs at AOBC?**  
Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth boxing and boxing training for kids programs focused on building discipline, coordination, and confidence for young athletes in Copenhagen.

**Q3: What types of sparring and competition training are available at AOBC?**  
We offer sparring classes and boxing competition training tailored for amateur and competitive boxers, including personal coaching to prepare you for your bouts safely and effectively.

**Q4: How does AOBC integrate fitness and boxing training?**  
AOBC combines boxing drills with fitness boxing and boxing workout plans to provide a dynamic workout experience that improves endurance, strength, and boxing skills for all levels.

**Q5: Who founded Ashira Oure Boxing Club and what is their background?**  
AOBC was founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, who brings his extensive experience and dedication to coaching boxers of all levels in Copenhagen.
