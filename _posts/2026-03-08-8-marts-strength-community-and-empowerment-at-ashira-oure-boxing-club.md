At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, 8 marts is more than just a date—it’s a celebration of strength, unity, and progress for every member of our Copenhagen boxing community. Whether you’re stepping into the ring for the first time or pushing your limits as a competitive athlete, AOBC stands with you, offering world-class training, encouragement, and a sense of belonging.

## Celebrating Growth and Empowerment at AOBC

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC is Copenhagen’s premier boxing gym, with locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund. On 8 marts, we shine a spotlight on the powerful spirit that drives our members—from enthusiastic beginners to seasoned competitors. Our club is built on three pillars:

- **Expert-led boxing classes** for all skill levels  
- **Fitness-focused workouts** that boost both body and mind  
- **A supportive, inclusive community** where everyone can thrive  

At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, we believe boxing is for everyone. No matter your background or fitness level, you’ll find a welcoming space to learn, grow, and challenge yourself.

## Boxing for All Levels and Ages

Whether you’re searching for a boxing club for beginners, looking to improve your technique, or preparing for competition, AOBC offers a wide range of classes and programs:

- **Beginner boxing classes:** Master the fundamentals in a friendly, encouraging environment  
- **Sparring and competition training:** Take your skills to the next level with structured, safe sparring sessions and expert guidance  
- **Youth and kids boxing:** Our dedicated youth programs introduce children and teens to the discipline and joy of boxing, fostering confidence and teamwork  
- **Personal coaching:** Get tailored support from experienced trainers, including Evans Ashira himself  

Our mission is to help you achieve your goals, whether that means building confidence, getting fit, or stepping into the ring for your first bout.

## Experience the AOBC Community

At AOBC, you’re not just joining a boxing gym—you’re becoming part of a community that values discipline, respect, and mutual support. Our members motivate each other, celebrate each other’s achievements, and grow together. On 8 marts, we’re reminded of the collective strength that comes from training side by side.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?  
AOBC provides beginner boxing classes designed to teach the fundamentals in a supportive and encouraging environment, perfect for those new to boxing in Copenhagen.

### Where are the Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?  
AOBC has two convenient gym locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, serving boxing enthusiasts throughout Copenhagen and surrounding areas.

### Does AOBC offer youth and kids boxing programs?  
Yes, Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers specialized youth boxing classes that focus on building confidence, discipline, and teamwork for children and teens.

### Can I get personal coaching from Evans Ashira at AOBC?  
Absolutely. AOBC offers personal coaching sessions, including training from founder and former world champion Evans Ashira, tailored to your individual goals.

### What types of boxing training programs are available at AOBC?  
AOBC offers a variety of programs including beginner classes, sparring sessions, competition training, fitness boxing, and amateur boxing club opportunities for all skill levels.
