Every year, the excitement of håndbold VM brings people together across Copenhagen, igniting a shared sense of passion, unity, and athletic excellence. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), we believe these same values make boxing not just a sport, but a powerful community experience—one that welcomes everyone, from first-timers to future champions.

## Why AOBC Is Copenhagen’s Home for Boxing and Fitness

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC stands out as the premier boxing gym in Copenhagen. With locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, our club is designed for everyone:

- **Beginners discovering boxing for the first time**
- **Fitness enthusiasts looking for a new challenge**
- **Youth and families seeking positive, disciplined activities**
- **Competitive boxers aiming for the next level**

Like the teams at håndbold VM, AOBC thrives on teamwork, discipline, and the drive to be your best. Our members support each other, celebrate every victory—big or small—and grow together as athletes and individuals.

## AOBC’s Programs for Every Level

Whether you’re searching for “boxing club for beginners” or looking to sharpen your skills in sparring classes, AOBC offers a range of expert-led programs:

- **Boxing Classes:** Taught by Evans Ashira and our experienced trainers, these sessions focus on technique, footwork, and fitness boxing.
- **Sparring & Competition Prep:** For those ready to step into the ring, our sparring classes and boxing competition training follow the highest boxing safety tips and rules.
- **Youth & Kids Boxing:** Our youth boxing and boxing training for kids programs create a positive space for young athletes to learn discipline, confidence, and teamwork—mirroring the camaraderie seen at håndbold VM.
- **Personal Coaching:** Customized plans and one-on-one coaching help every member achieve their unique goals, from fitness boxing to competing at an amateur boxing club level.

## A Supportive Boxing Community in Copenhagen

AOBC is more than just a boxing gym—it’s a community. Our members come from across Copenhagen, drawn by the welcoming atmosphere and the drive to improve together. Whether you’re searching for “boxing classes near me,” “hellerup boxing gym,” or “copenhagen boxing club,” you’ll find a home at AOBC.

Here’s what sets us apart:

- **Inclusive Environment:** Everyone, regardless of experience, is encouraged to join and grow.
- **Expert Guidance:** Learn from Evans Ashira, Yuna Ashira, Lucas Ashira, and our dedicated coaching team.
- **Holistic Wellness:** We believe in the full spectrum of health—combining oure fitness, ashira wellness, and the discipline of boxing.

## Ready for Your First Boxing Class?

Feeling inspired by the energy of håndbold VM? There’s no better time to experience the difference at Ashira Oure Boxing Club. Whether you want to build strength, gain confidence, or join a thriving boxing community, AOBC welcomes you.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?  
AOBC offers a variety of boxing classes including beginner boxing classes, sparring classes, competition training, youth boxing, and personal coaching, all led by experienced trainers like Evans Ashira.

### Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?  
AOBC has two main locations in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, making it convenient for members across the city and surrounding areas.

### Can beginners join Ashira Oure Boxing Club?  
Absolutely! AOBC welcomes beginners with tailored beginner boxing classes designed to teach you the fundamentals of boxing, fitness, and discipline in a supportive environment.

### Does AOBC offer boxing programs for kids and youth?  
Yes, AOBC provides youth boxing and boxing training for kids programs focused on developing confidence, teamwork, and discipline in a positive and safe environment.

### How can I prepare for my first boxing class at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?  
It’s best to wear comfortable workout clothing and bring water. AOBC’s trainers will guide you through a beginner-friendly boxing workout plan, focusing on technique, fitness boxing, and safety tips to ensure a great first experience.
