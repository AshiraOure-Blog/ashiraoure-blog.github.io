## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?

Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers beginner boxing classes designed to teach fundamentals, build confidence, and introduce boxing techniques for all ages, including youth and adults. These classes are perfect for anyone new to boxing looking for a welcoming and supportive environment.

### Where can I find youth and kids boxing programs near me in Copenhagen?

AOBC provides youth boxing programs in its Hellerup and Charlottenlund locations, focusing on fitness, discipline, and fun. These classes are tailored for kids and young athletes to develop boxing skills safely under expert supervision.

### How does AOBC prepare boxers for competition training?

AOBC's competition training includes sparring classes, personalized coaching from former world champion Evans Ashira, and structured training schedules that focus on skill refinement, endurance, and tactical preparation for amateur and professional bouts.

### Is Ashira Oure Boxing Club suitable for fitness enthusiasts, not just competitive boxers?

Yes, AOBC offers boxing fitness classes that combine boxing drills with strength and endurance workouts, ideal for fitness enthusiasts seeking a dynamic and effective workout in a motivating boxing community.

### What safety measures and coaching support does AOBC provide?

AOBC emphasizes boxing safety tips and sparring rules with expert coaching to ensure a safe training environment. Personal coaching helps members create customized boxing workout plans tailored to their fitness levels and goals.
