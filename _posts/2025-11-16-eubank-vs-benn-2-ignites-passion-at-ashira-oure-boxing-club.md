- **Expert Coaching:** Led by Evans Ashira and a dedicated team, our classes are built on world-class expertise.
- **Supportive Community:** Members encourage each other through every boxing workout, fitness drill, and sparring round.
- **Youth and Family Programs:** AOBC is proud to offer youth boxing and kids boxing near you, helping young athletes build confidence and discipline.

### 2. **Personal Coaching and Competition Prep**

Evans Ashira’s championship experience shapes every training plan, including tailored coaching for amateur and competitive boxers. If you’re dreaming of a title bout—or just want to push your limits—AOBC is your boxing gym in Copenhagen.

### 3. **Youth Boxing and Community Spirit**

Our youth boxing academy empowers the next generation with discipline, fitness, and self-belief. AOBC’s community spirit ensures everyone, from young beginners to lifelong fighters, feels welcome and motivated.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?

AOBC provides beginner boxing classes designed to teach the fundamentals such as stance, footwork, and punching technique. These classes are perfect for newcomers to boxing and fitness enthusiasts in Copenhagen.

### How can I join youth boxing programs at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?

AOBC offers youth boxing and kids boxing near you with specialized programs focusing on confidence, discipline, and fitness. You can enroll your child by contacting AOBC directly through their website or visiting their gyms in Hellerup or Charlottenlund.

### What types of sparring and competition training are available at AOBC?

Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers sparring classes and boxing competition training tailored for amateur and competitive boxers. Under the guidance of Evans Ashira and expert coaches, you will receive personalized coaching to prepare for fights safely and effectively.

### Is Ashira Oure Boxing Club suitable for fitness boxing in Copenhagen?

Yes, AOBC provides fitness boxing classes that combine cardio workouts with boxing drills to improve both skill and overall fitness. These sessions are suitable for all fitness levels looking for an engaging gym with boxing.

### Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?

AOBC has two convenient locations in Copenhagen: Hellerup Boxing Gym and Charlottenlund. Both locations offer comprehensive boxing training, classes for beginners, youth programs, and personal coaching.
