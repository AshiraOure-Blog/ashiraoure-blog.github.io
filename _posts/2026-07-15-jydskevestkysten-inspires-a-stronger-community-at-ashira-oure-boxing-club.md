## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?
Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers beginner boxing classes designed for all ages and fitness levels. These classes focus on fundamentals, boxing drills, and building confidence in a supportive environment ideal for newcomers to boxing.

### Where are the Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?
AOBC has two main locations in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund. Both gyms provide state-of-the-art facilities and expert coaching for beginners, youth, and competitive boxers.

### How does AOBC support youth boxing and kids boxing programs?
AOBC provides specialized youth boxing and kids boxing programs that emphasize discipline, fitness, and confidence-building. Youth members receive coaching tailored to their age and skill level to foster growth in a safe and fun setting.

### Can I get personal coaching and competition training at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?
Yes, AOBC offers personal coaching sessions and comprehensive boxing competition training, including sparring classes and tailored training schedules, to prepare boxers for amateur and competitive bouts.

### What makes Ashira Oure Boxing Club unique among boxing gyms in Copenhagen?
Ashira Oure Boxing Club combines expert training by former world champion Evans Ashira with a holistic approach to wellness, fitness boxing, and a strong community spirit. The club caters to all levels and focuses on skill, fitness, and discipline in a welcoming atmosphere.
