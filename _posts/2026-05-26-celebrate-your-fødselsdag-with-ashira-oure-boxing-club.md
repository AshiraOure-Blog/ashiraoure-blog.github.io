## Make Your Birthday Count at Copenhagen’s Premier Boxing Gym

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC) welcomes everyone, from absolute beginners to competitive boxers. With locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, AOBC is more than a boxing gym—it’s a place where your personal victories matter, both inside and outside the ring.

Whether you’re searching for a unique way to celebrate your fødselsdag or looking to kickstart your fitness journey, our expert-led boxing classes and fitness workouts offer something for everyone. Here’s how you can make your next birthday unforgettable at AOBC:

- **Group Boxing Classes:** Gather your friends or family and experience a dynamic beginner boxing class together. Our supportive coaches ensure everyone, regardless of skill level, feels empowered and motivated.
- **Personal Coaching:** Treat yourself to a one-on-one session with a professional trainer. AOBC’s personal coaching is tailored to your goals, making it a fantastic way to test your limits and build confidence.
- **Youth & Kids Programs:** Give young boxers a chance to celebrate their fødselsdag with fun, safe, and energetic youth boxing sessions. Our kids boxing programs are designed to encourage discipline, teamwork, and healthy habits.
- **Fitness and Sparring:** For those seeking a challenge, try our sparring classes or fitness boxing workouts. Experience the thrill of competition in a controlled, friendly environment.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?  
Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers beginner boxing classes tailored for all ages and fitness levels at their locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund. These classes focus on fundamentals, fitness boxing, and building confidence in a supportive community.

### Can kids join boxing training at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?  
Yes, AOBC provides specialized youth and kids boxing programs designed to teach discipline, teamwork, and boxing skills in a fun and safe environment, perfect for children looking to start boxing in Copenhagen.

### How can I book a personal coaching session at AOBC?  
You can easily book a one-on-one personal coaching session with professional trainers at Ashira Oure Boxing Club by visiting their website or contacting their Copenhagen locations directly.

### Does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer sparring and competition training?  
Yes, AOBC offers sparring classes and boxing competition training designed for amateur boxers and those preparing for competitive bouts, focusing on safety, technique, and strategy.

### Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s gyms located in Copenhagen?  
Ashira Oure Boxing Club has two convenient locations: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, serving anyone searching for a “boxing club Copenhagen” or “Hellerup boxing gym.”
