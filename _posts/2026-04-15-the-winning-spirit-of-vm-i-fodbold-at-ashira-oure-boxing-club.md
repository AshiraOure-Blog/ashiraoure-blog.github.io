## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
AOBC provides beginner boxing classes tailored for those new to the sport. These classes focus on fundamentals, boxing drills, and fitness boxing techniques, making them ideal for anyone searching for a boxing club for beginners or first boxing class experiences in Copenhagen.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?**  
The club has two main locations: a boxing gym in Hellerup and another facility in Charlottenlund. Both are easily accessible and serve as central hubs for boxing training, youth programs, sparring classes, and competition prep.

**Q3: Does Ashira Oure offer youth and kids boxing programs?**  
Yes, AOBC has specialized youth boxing and kids boxing classes designed to teach discipline, teamwork, and boxing safety tips. These programs are led by experienced trainers including Evans Ashira and focus on building confidence and skill in young athletes.

**Q4: Can I get personal coaching or competition training at AOBC?**  
Absolutely. Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers personal coaching tailored to individual goals, as well as boxing competition training and sparring classes to help amateur boxers prepare for local and national events.

**Q5: What makes Ashira Oure Boxing Club unique among Copenhagen boxing gyms?**  
AOBC stands out due to its combination of expert-led classes, a supportive boxing community, comprehensive training programs, and the legacy of founder and former world champion Evans Ashira. The club emphasizes both fitness and skill development in a motivating environment.
