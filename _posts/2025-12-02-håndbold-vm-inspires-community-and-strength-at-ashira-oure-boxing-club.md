Every year, the buzz of håndbold vm brings energy, teamwork, and a sense of unity to Copenhagen. It’s a powerful reminder of what dedicated training, discipline, and community can achieve—not just on the handball court, but in every sport. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, we embrace these same values, creating an inclusive environment where everyone—from complete beginners to competitive boxers—can thrive.

## Building Champions at Copenhagen’s Top Boxing Gym

Just like the teams at håndbold vm, AOBC thrives on passion and purpose. Whether you’re stepping into the ring for your first boxing class or preparing for a competition, our club welcomes you. Our expert coaches, led by Evans Ashira, guide you through structured programs focused on skill, fitness, and discipline.

### Why Choose Ashira Oure Boxing Club?

- **All Levels Welcome:** We offer beginner boxing classes, youth boxing sessions, and advanced competition training. At AOBC, everyone finds their place—kids, teens, adults, and seasoned athletes.
- **Community Spirit:** Like håndbold vm teams, our members support and motivate each other. The AOBC family is known for its positive energy and camaraderie, making it the premier boxing club Copenhagen has to offer.
- **Expert Coaching:** Evans Ashira and our team of skilled trainers ensure every session is safe, effective, and tailored to your goals.
- **Comprehensive Programs:** From boxing fitness and sparring classes to personal coaching and youth programs, AOBC covers every aspect of boxing training.

## The AOBC Experience: More Than Just Boxing

At AOBC, we believe that sport is about more than physical fitness—it’s about building confidence, resilience, and community. Our gyms in Hellerup and Charlottenlund are designed to be welcoming spaces for everyone, whether you’re a parent searching for “kids boxing near me” or an athlete preparing for your next match.

Our approach blends the competitive spirit seen at håndbold vm with personal growth. Members benefit from:

- **Structured Boxing Workout Plans**
- **Boxing and Fitness Classes**
- **Safe Sparring Sessions**
- **Youth and Kids Programs** focused on skill and self-esteem
- **Personal Coaching** from Evans Ashira and team

## Join the Movement in Copenhagen

The excitement of håndbold vm reminds us that sport brings people together—and at AOBC, we do just that every day. Whether you’re curious about your first boxing class or ready to sharpen your competition edge, Ashira Oure Boxing Club is your home for fitness, skill, and community.

Ready to start your journey? [Book your first class with AOBC today](https://www.ashiraoure.com/) and find out why we’re Copenhagen’s premier boxing gym.

At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, everyone is welcome. Train like a champion, grow with a supportive community, and experience the spirit of teamwork that makes håndbold vm so inspiring. Join us in Hellerup or Charlottenlund—your future in boxing and fitness starts here.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?
AOBC provides beginner boxing classes designed to introduce newcomers to boxing techniques, fitness boxing, and safety tips. Classes cater to various age groups, including kids, teens, and adults, making it ideal for anyone searching for a boxing gym in Copenhagen or “kids boxing near me.”

### Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?
Ashira Oure Boxing Club has two convenient locations in Copenhagen: Hellerup and Charlottenlund. Both gyms offer comprehensive boxing training, sparring classes, youth programs, and fitness boxing sessions.

### How does Ashira Oure Boxing Club support youth boxing and kids training?
AOBC offers specialized youth and kids boxing programs focused on skill development, discipline, and confidence-building. These programs are tailored to beginners and aim to encourage healthy fitness habits and personal growth.

### Who is Evans Ashira and what role does he play at AOBC?
Evans Ashira is a former world boxing champion and the founder of Ashira Oure Boxing Club. He leads the expert coaching team, ensuring all training sessions prioritize skill, discipline, and safety for all members.

### Can I train for boxing competitions at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?
Yes, AOBC offers advanced competition training and sparring classes for amateur and competitive boxers. Their programs include boxing workout plans and training schedules tailored to prepare you for boxing competition training and matches.
