Every summer, grøn koncert brings the Copenhagen community together for music, movement, and unforgettable experiences. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, we believe in the same spirit—bringing people from all backgrounds together, inspiring growth, and celebrating the joy of movement in a supportive environment.

## Discover Boxing and Community at AOBC in Copenhagen

Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC) stands out as Copenhagen’s premier **boxing gym Copenhagen**, with convenient locations in **Hellerup boxing gym** and Charlottenlund. Founded by former world champion **Evans Ashira**, our club opens its doors to everyone—from complete beginners to competitive athletes—united by a passion for **boxing Copenhagen**, fitness, and discipline.

Like grøn koncert, AOBC is about more than just the action in the ring. It’s about community, connection, and creating memories that last. Whether you’re searching for a **boxing club for beginners** or looking to challenge yourself at a top-tier **boxing academy**, you’ll find your place here among the best **boxing club Copenhagen** offers.

## Why Choose Ashira Oure Boxing Club?

### Expert Coaching for All Levels in Copenhagen

Our classes are led by boxing professionals, including **Evans Ashira** himself. We offer:

- **Beginner boxing classes**: Build confidence, learn fundamentals, and get fit in a friendly setting.
- **Youth boxing and kids boxing near me**: Fun, safe, and structured programs for children and teens in Copenhagen.
- **Competition training and sparring classes**: For those ready to push limits and step into the ring.
- **Personal coaching**: Tailored programs for individual goals and skill levels.

### Fitness, Skill, and Discipline at AOBC

Boxing at AOBC isn’t just about punches—it’s a full-body workout that strengthens body and mind. Our **boxing fitness** and **boxing and fitness classes** blend technique with high-energy workouts, helping you:

- Boost cardiovascular health
- Build strength and endurance
- Sharpen focus and discipline
- Relieve stress and find community

### A Welcoming Boxing Community in Copenhagen

Much like the inclusive atmosphere at grøn koncert, AOBC is proud of its diverse and supportive **boxing community**. Our members motivate each other, celebrate achievements, and build friendships that go beyond the gym. Whether you’re attending your **first boxing class** or preparing for a competition, you’ll find encouragement every step of the way.

### Safety and Progress in Boxing Training

Your well-being is our priority. We teach **boxing safety tips**, proper technique, and the best **boxing workout plan** for your ability level. From youth programs to advanced sparring, every session is designed to help you train safely and effectively.

## Join the AOBC Movement in Copenhagen

Experience the difference of a **Copenhagen boxing club** that truly cares—where every punch, every class, and every member matters.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners?

Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers beginner boxing classes designed to help you build confidence, learn fundamental techniques, and improve fitness in a friendly and supportive environment. These classes are perfect for those new to boxing and are held at our Hellerup and Charlottenlund locations in Copenhagen.

### Is there a youth or kids boxing program at AOBC?

Yes, AOBC provides structured youth boxing programs, including kids boxing classes near you in Copenhagen. These programs focus on safety, skill development, and fun, promoting discipline and fitness for children and teenagers.

### Can I get personal coaching at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?

Absolutely. AOBC offers personal coaching tailored to your individual goals and skill level, whether you're training for fitness, sparring, or competition preparation, all guided by expert coaches including former world champion Evans Ashira.

### What makes Ashira Oure Boxing Club different from other boxing gyms in Copenhagen?

AOBC combines expert coaching, a welcoming community, and a focus on both fitness and discipline. Founded by Evans Ashira, AOBC emphasizes safe training, personalized programs, and a supportive atmosphere that nurtures beginners and competitive boxers alike.

### How do I prepare for my first boxing class at AOBC?

For your first boxing class, wear comfortable workout clothes and bring a water bottle. AOBC instructors will guide you through basic boxing drills, safety tips, and techniques, ensuring a positive and motivating introduction to boxing.
