When you think of legendary teams like Arsenal FC, you picture passion, discipline, and unity—qualities that also define Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), Copenhagen’s premier boxing gym founded by former world champion Evans Ashira. Just as Arsenal FC inspires fans and athletes worldwide, AOBC inspires those in Copenhagen to discover their own strength, develop their skills, and become champions—inside and outside the ring.

#### From Beginners to Champions: Everyone Belongs

Whether you’re walking into your first boxing class or gearing up for serious competition, AOBC welcomes you. Our Hellerup and Charlottenlund locations offer a variety of boxing and fitness classes designed for all levels. Beginners find a supportive community that takes the intimidation out of starting something new, while competitive boxers push their limits surrounded by the experience of Evans Ashira and his skilled team.

**Why choose Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**

- Expert-led beginner boxing classes and advanced training  
- Personal coaching tailored for your unique goals  
- Sparring classes and competition prep in a safe, encouraging environment  
- Youth boxing programs and kids boxing near me for young champions  
- Comprehensive fitness boxing and boxing workout plans for all levels  
- Focus on discipline, respect, and community  

#### Building Strength, Confidence, and Community

AOBC is more than a boxing gym—it’s a place where people from all walks of life come together, united by a shared commitment to growth. Our members aren’t just training their bodies—they’re building mental toughness, confidence, and lifelong friendships. Guided by Evans Ashira, our community is driven by the same determination and teamwork that make clubs like Arsenal FC legendary.

- **Youth Boxing & Kids’ Classes:** Our youth programs teach kids more than just boxing skills—they learn discipline, respect, and resilience. With experienced coaches like Yuna Ashira and Lucas Ashira, every young boxer is supported on their journey.  
- **Fitness and Wellness:** Looking for oure fitness and boxing training for kids? AOBC combines the best of boxing and fitness, with classes designed to improve endurance, strength, and overall well-being.  
- **Safe, Structured Training:** Our coaches ensure every session follows boxing safety tips and sparring rules, so members can train hard and smart.  

#### Your Pathway to Excellence

At AOBC, your journey is personal. Whether your goal is to compete at the highest levels or simply to feel stronger and more confident, our team is here to help you build your own winning mindset—just like the stars of Arsenal FC. From your first boxing class to advanced sparring sessions, you’ll find expert guidance, encouragement, and a community that celebrates every achievement.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC offers expert-led beginner boxing classes in both Hellerup and Charlottenlund, designed to introduce new boxers to fundamental techniques, fitness boxing, and safe sparring practices within a supportive community.

**Q2: Are there youth boxing programs available at AOBC?**  
A2: Yes, AOBC provides youth boxing classes focused on discipline, respect, and skill development. Experienced coaches like Yuna Ashira and Lucas Ashira guide kids through age-appropriate training to build confidence and resilience.

**Q3: How does AOBC support competitive boxing training in Copenhagen?**  
A3: AOBC offers sparring classes, boxing competition training, and personal coaching tailored to prepare amateur and competitive boxers for local and international bouts, emphasizing technique, fitness, and boxing safety rules.

**Q4: Can I combine fitness training with boxing at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**  
A4: Absolutely! AOBC integrates boxing drills with fitness workouts, providing comprehensive boxing fitness classes and boxing workout plans that improve endurance, strength, and overall wellness.

**Q5: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?**  
A5: AOBC operates two main gyms located in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, making it accessible for Copenhagen residents seeking top-tier boxing and fitness training.
