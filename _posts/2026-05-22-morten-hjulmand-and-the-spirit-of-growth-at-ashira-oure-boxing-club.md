## Your First Steps Into Boxing at AOBC

Taking your first boxing class can be transformative. At AOBC, we make sure every new member feels welcome and empowered, just as morten hjulmand inspires newcomers in his field. Our beginner boxing classes focus on:

- **Fundamentals:** Learn basic boxing techniques, boxing drills, and footwork in a safe environment.
- **Fitness:** Experience the benefits of boxing fitness—improved strength, endurance, and confidence.
- **Safety:** We prioritize boxing safety tips and clear boxing sparring rules to ensure every session is enjoyable and secure.

If you’re searching for “boxing training for kids” or “kids boxing near me” in Copenhagen, our youth programs are designed to build discipline, coordination, and self-esteem from an early age.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners?

AOBC offers beginner boxing classes focused on fundamentals, including basic boxing techniques, drills, footwork, and boxing fitness to build strength and confidence safely.

### Where are the Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?

AOBC has two convenient locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, serving the Copenhagen area with top-tier boxing training and fitness programs.

### Does AOBC provide youth and kids boxing programs?

Yes, Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers specialized youth boxing classes designed to develop discipline, coordination, and self-esteem for kids and young athletes in Copenhagen.

### Can I train for competitive boxing at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?

Absolutely. AOBC provides advanced sparring classes, personalized boxing training schedules, and competition prep guided by world champion Evans Ashira to help you excel in amateur boxing competitions.

### How does Ashira Oure Boxing Club ensure safety during boxing training?

Safety is a priority at AOBC. The club enforces clear boxing sparring rules and provides boxing safety tips to create a secure and supportive environment for all members.
