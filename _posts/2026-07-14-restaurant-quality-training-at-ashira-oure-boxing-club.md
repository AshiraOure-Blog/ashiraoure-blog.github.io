At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, we believe your training experience should be as memorable and refined as dining at Copenhagen’s best restaurant—crafted with expertise, care, and a dash of inspiration. AOBC, founded by world champion Evans Ashira, stands as the premier boxing club in Copenhagen, with locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund. Whether you're a complete beginner, fitness enthusiast, youth participant, or competitive boxer, our community welcomes you to a menu of classes and programs designed to help you thrive.

## An Inclusive Boxing Community for All Levels in Copenhagen

Ashira Oure Boxing Club is more than a boxing gym—it's a place where every member feels valued and supported. Like a great restaurant that caters to diverse tastes, AOBC offers a range of training options:

- **Beginner boxing classes** for those new to the sport  
- **Boxing and fitness classes** blending skill training with full-body workouts  
- **Youth boxing and kids boxing near me** programs, fostering discipline and confidence in young athletes  
- **Sparring classes** and **boxing competition training** for those aiming higher  
- **Personal coaching** that tailors each session to your unique goals and abilities  

Our coaches, led by Evans Ashira, provide expert guidance that meets you where you are—ensuring everyone, from curious newcomers to seasoned fighters, feels empowered to take the next step.

## Skill, Fitness, and Discipline—Served Fresh Every Day at AOBC

Training at AOBC is about more than just throwing punches. Like a master chef balancing flavors, our approach blends skill development, fitness, and discipline. Every class is thoughtfully structured:

- Warm-ups and technical **boxing drills**  
- Cardio and strength-building routines for peak **boxing fitness**  
- Controlled sparring with emphasis on **boxing safety tips** and respect  
- Individual feedback to hone your technique—from your **first boxing class** onward  

Whether you train at our Hellerup boxing gym or the Charlottenlund location, you'll find a welcoming space dedicated to growth and camaraderie.

## Designed for Copenhagen’s Active Boxing Community

Copenhagen’s energy is reflected in our members—a mix of students, professionals, parents, and competitors, all united by a passion for self-improvement. AOBC’s programs are ideal for:

- Those searching for a **boxing club for beginners** or an **amateur boxing club**  
- Parents seeking **boxing training for kids** in a safe, responsible environment  
- Fitness lovers wanting to elevate their routine with **boxing and fitness classes**  
- Athletes preparing for the next level—whether local tournaments or international competitions  

We also embrace the broader goals of health and wellness, drawing inspiration from the Ashira family legacy, including Yuna Ashira, Lucas Ashira, and Mino Ashira, and the values of **ashira wellness** and **oure fitness**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners in Copenhagen?
Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers beginner boxing classes designed to introduce newcomers to the fundamentals of boxing. These classes focus on skill development, fitness, and discipline, with personalized coaching to ensure a safe and supportive learning environment.

### Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?
AOBC has two convenient locations in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund. Both gyms provide full access to boxing training, sparring classes, and fitness programs.

### Can children join boxing training at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?
Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth boxing and kids boxing programs that promote discipline, confidence, and physical fitness in a safe and structured environment ideal for young athletes.

### Does Ashira Oure Boxing Club provide training for competitive boxing and sparring?
Absolutely. AOBC provides sparring classes and boxing competition training tailored for those preparing for amateur and professional fights, with expert coaching led by former world champion Evans Ashira.

### How can I book my first boxing class at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?
You can easily book your first class or learn more about AOBC’s programs by visiting their official website at [https://www.ashiraoure.com/](https://www.ashiraoure.com/).
