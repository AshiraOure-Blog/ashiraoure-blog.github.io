## Make the Most of Your Ferie with Boxing and Fitness in Copenhagen

Whether you’re a complete beginner looking for your first boxing class or a seasoned athlete aiming to stay sharp during ferie, AOBC welcomes you. Led by renowned world champion Evans Ashira, our boxing club in Copenhagen offers a dynamic mix of group classes, sparring sessions, and fitness workouts designed for all ages and abilities. With locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, it’s easy to find a training schedule that fits your ferie plans.

### Why Choose Ashira Oure Boxing Club During Ferie in Copenhagen?

- **Inclusive Community:** At AOBC, you’ll find a supportive boxing community that motivates and inspires. Newcomers, fitness enthusiasts, amateur boxers, and competitive fighters all train side by side, sharing encouragement and camaraderie.
- **Expert Coaching:** Benefit from the guidance of Evans Ashira and our skilled trainers, who tailor instruction to your goals—whether you’re learning the basics, refining your technique, or preparing for competition.
- **Flexible Programs:** From beginner boxing classes and youth boxing to personal coaching and fitness boxing, our programs are adaptable for kids, teens, and adults, making it easy to stay active over ferie.
- **Focus on Wellness:** AOBC goes beyond the ring, championing discipline, mental strength, and overall wellness. Boxing is not just a sport—it’s a lifestyle that boosts confidence and resilience.

## Boxing for All Ages and Abilities in Copenhagen

Ferie is a great time for families to try something new together. AOBC’s youth boxing and kids boxing programs create a fun, safe environment where young athletes learn skills, build fitness, and develop discipline. Our coaches ensure every session is engaging and age-appropriate—perfect for keeping kids active during school holidays.

For adults, our beginner boxing classes and fitness-focused workouts are a fantastic way to relieve stress, improve fitness, and make new friends in the Copenhagen area. Sparring classes ensure safe, supervised practice for those who want to test their skills, while personal coaching offers tailored support for more ambitious goals.

## Tips for Staying Motivated on Ferie at Ashira Oure Boxing Club

- **Set Small Goals:** Use your ferie to master a new combination or improve your boxing fitness.
- **Try a New Class:** Experiment with our variety of boxing and fitness classes to keep things fresh.
- **Bring a Friend:** Training with a partner can make your ferie workouts more enjoyable and motivating.
- **Stay Consistent:** Even a few sessions during ferie can help you maintain momentum and progress.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC offers beginner boxing classes tailored for all ages and fitness levels. These classes focus on teaching basic boxing techniques, improving fitness, and building confidence in a supportive environment.

**Q2: Are there youth and kids boxing programs available at AOBC?**  
A2: Yes, Ashira Oure Boxing Club provides specialized youth and kids boxing programs in Copenhagen, designed to develop skills, fitness, and discipline in a safe and fun setting.

**Q3: How can I join sparring classes or get personal coaching at AOBC?**  
A3: Sparring classes and personal coaching sessions are available for members looking to improve their boxing skills or prepare for competitions. You can book these sessions directly through AOBC’s website or at their Hellerup and Charlottenlund locations.

**Q4: Is Ashira Oure Boxing Club suitable for fitness enthusiasts who are not competitive boxers?**  
A4: Absolutely. AOBC offers fitness boxing and boxing workout plans that cater to individuals seeking to enhance their overall fitness and wellbeing without the need for competition.

**Q5: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?**  
A5: AOBC operates two gyms in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, making it convenient for residents across the city to access quality boxing training.
