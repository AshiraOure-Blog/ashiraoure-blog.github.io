Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC) is more than a boxing gym in Copenhagen; it’s a community where commitment overcomes any obstacle, including unpredictable Danish weather. Whether you’re stepping into the ring for your first beginner boxing class, preparing for competition, or just seeking a new fitness challenge, our doors in Hellerup and Charlottenlund are always open.

### Boxing Indoors, Resilience Outdoors at AOBC

Our expert-led boxing classes are designed for all levels, from absolute beginners to seasoned fighters. As Copenhagen’s premier boxing gym founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC knows that true discipline is forged in every season. The weather outside might influence your commute, but it will never affect the energy and warmth inside our club.

- **Beginner boxing classes**: Learn the basics in a safe, supportive setting.
- **Fitness boxing sessions**: Boost your cardio, strength, and agility—rain or shine.
- **Sparring and competition prep**: Test your skills and grow your confidence under expert guidance.
- **Youth and kids boxing**: Inspiring the next generation of athletes with programs tailored for young boxers.

## Weathering Challenges, Achieving Goals at Ashira Oure Boxing Club

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners?**  
A1: AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed to teach fundamental techniques in a safe and supportive environment. These classes focus on building skills, fitness, and confidence for newcomers to boxing.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?**  
A2: Ashira Oure Boxing Club has two main locations in Copenhagen: Hellerup and Charlottenlund, providing easy access for residents in and around the city.

**Q3: Does AOBC provide boxing training for kids and youth?**  
A3: Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth and kids boxing programs to promote fitness, discipline, and skill development in a fun and supportive setting.

**Q4: How does AOBC accommodate training during harsh weather in Copenhagen?**  
A4: AOBC’s indoor facilities and flexible training schedules ensure that all members can train effectively regardless of weather conditions, maintaining focus on fitness and skill development year-round.

**Q5: Can I prepare for boxing competitions at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**  
A5: Absolutely. AOBC provides sparring classes and competition preparation programs led by experienced coaches, including former world champion Evans Ashira, to help boxers excel in amateur and professional competitions.
