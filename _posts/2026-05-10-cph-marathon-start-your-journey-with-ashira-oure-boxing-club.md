### What Sets AOBC Apart as a Boxing Club in Copenhagen?

- **Expert-Led Training:** Every class at AOBC, from beginner boxing classes to advanced sparring sessions, is guided by skilled coaches who know what it takes to excel. Evans Ashira and his team are dedicated to helping you master technique, build confidence, and reach your goals.
- **All Levels Welcome:** You don’t need any experience to start. Our boxing club for beginners in Copenhagen offers a safe, encouraging space to learn the basics of boxing, fitness, and discipline.
- **Holistic Fitness and Boxing Training:** Boxing is more than just throwing punches. Our boxing and fitness classes are designed to boost your cardio, strength, coordination, and mental focus—skills that support marathon running and everyday life.
- **Youth and Kids Boxing Programs in Copenhagen:** AOBC is a proud home to youth boxing and kids boxing near me in Copenhagen. We nurture young talent, teaching discipline, respect, and teamwork in a fun environment.
- **Community Spirit:** Whether you train at our Hellerup boxing gym or Charlottenlund location, you’ll find a welcoming boxing community that celebrates every victory—big or small.

## From Marathon Start to Boxing Ring in Copenhagen

What do marathoners and boxers have in common? Both require determination, a strong mindset, and a commitment to regular training. At AOBC, we help you build these qualities. Our boxing workout plans, sparring classes, and personal coaching sessions are perfect cross-training for runners, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone looking to break new ground.

### How Ashira Oure Boxing Club Supports Your Fitness Journey in Copenhagen

- **Personal Coaching:** Get tailored boxing training schedules and one-on-one attention to accelerate your progress.
- **Structured Boxing Classes:** Join group sessions that focus on fitness boxing, technical drills, and sparring—ideal for improving stamina, agility, and self-confidence.
- **Competition Preparation:** Dreaming of stepping into the ring or pushing your limits? Our boxing competition training and amateur boxing club environment prepare you for the next level.
- **Safe, Supportive Environment:** AOBC prioritizes safety, offering boxing safety tips and guidance to ensure every member can train confidently.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?  
AOBC offers beginner boxing classes tailored to those new to the sport, focusing on fundamentals, fitness, and building confidence in a supportive environment.

### Where are the Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?  
AOBC has two main locations: the Hellerup boxing gym and the Charlottenlund facility, both serving the Copenhagen area with comprehensive boxing programs.

### Can children join youth boxing programs at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?  
Yes, AOBC provides specialized youth and kids boxing classes designed to teach discipline, fitness, and teamwork for young boxers in Copenhagen.

### How can boxing training at AOBC benefit marathon runners?  
Boxing training improves cardio, strength, coordination, and mental toughness, making it an excellent cross-training option for marathon runners and fitness enthusiasts.

### Does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer personal coaching and competition training?  
Yes, AOBC provides personalized coaching and dedicated boxing competition training to help members prepare for amateur bouts and enhance their skills.
