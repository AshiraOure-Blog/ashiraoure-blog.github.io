## The Marathon Mindset Meets Boxing Excellence in Copenhagen

Training for the København Marathon is about more than running. It’s about discipline, commitment, and the support of a like-minded community—values that Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC) champions every day. Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC is Copenhagen’s leading boxing gym, dedicated to helping everyone from beginners to competitive athletes grow stronger together.

### Why Combine Marathon Training with Boxing at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?

Boxing and marathon preparation have more in common than you might think. Both require stamina, mental fortitude, and a structured approach to training. Here’s how AOBC can help you complement your running with top-tier boxing and fitness in Copenhagen:

- **Total Body Fitness:** Our boxing classes and fitness workouts target every muscle group, improving strength, agility, and cardiovascular endurance—perfect for marathon runners and fitness enthusiasts.
- **Expert Coaching:** Train with experienced coaches like Evans Ashira, who bring world-class knowledge to every session. Whether you’re learning your first jab or sharpening your technique, you’ll benefit from personalized attention.
- **Supportive Community:** AOBC’s diverse, inclusive environment ensures you’ll find encouragement and motivation at every step, just like the cheering crowds along the marathon route.
- **Discipline and Routine:** Our structured boxing training schedules and workout plans help you stay disciplined, focused, and on track to achieve your goals—both in the ring and on the road.

## Accessible Boxing and Fitness Programs for All Levels in Copenhagen

Whether you’re a seasoned marathoner, a beginner runner, or completely new to fitness, Ashira Oure Boxing Club welcomes you. Our Copenhagen locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund offer:

- **Beginner Boxing Classes:** Learn the basics in a safe, supportive environment—no experience needed.
- **Fitness Boxing Sessions:** Focus on stamina, strength, and technique, ideal for cross-training with your marathon prep.
- **Youth Boxing and Kids Programs:** AOBC is proud to offer youth boxing and kids boxing near me, fostering confidence and healthy habits from an early age.
- **Sparring and Competition Prep:** For those looking to push their limits, our sparring classes and boxing competition training provide the ultimate challenge.
- **Personal Coaching:** Get one-on-one guidance from experts like Evans Ashira to customize your boxing and fitness journey.
