## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners in Copenhagen?

Ashira Oure Boxing Club provides beginner boxing classes designed to introduce new members to the fundamentals of boxing, including basic drills, techniques, and fitness training in a supportive environment ideal for those new to the sport.

### Where are the Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?

AOBC has two convenient locations in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, making it accessible for residents across the Copenhagen metropolitan area.

### Does AOBC offer youth and kids boxing programs?

Yes, Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers specialized youth boxing and kids boxing programs focused on skill development, fitness, discipline, and building confidence in a safe and encouraging setting.

### Can I prepare for boxing competitions at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?

Absolutely. AOBC provides sparring classes and boxing competition training led by experienced coaches, including world champion Evans Ashira, to help boxers of all levels prepare for amateur and competitive boxing events.

### How can I book my first boxing class at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?
