On Grundlovsdag, Denmark celebrates the values of unity, freedom, and progress—principles that are at the heart of Ashira Oure Boxing Club. Whether you are stepping into the ring for the first time or you’re a seasoned competitor, AOBC in Hellerup and Charlottenlund is Copenhagen’s premier destination for boxing excellence and genuine community.

#### The Spirit of Grundlovsdag at Ashira Oure Boxing Club in Copenhagen

Grundlovsdag is all about coming together, setting goals, and pushing boundaries. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, founded by world champion Evans Ashira, every member is encouraged to strive for their personal best. AOBC welcomes everyone from curious beginners searching for their first boxing class to competitive fighters aiming for their next title.

**What makes AOBC stand out as a boxing club in Copenhagen?**

- **Expert-Led Classes:** Learn from experienced coaches, including Evans Ashira himself, who bring championship knowledge and motivating energy to every session.
- **Supportive Community:** AOBC is more than a boxing gym in Copenhagen—it’s a family. Members support each other through every punch, combination, and milestone.
- **Inclusive Programs:** With beginner boxing classes, youth boxing, sparring sessions, and fitness boxing, there’s a place for everyone at AOBC.
- **Discipline and Growth:** Boxing is about more than physical strength. AOBC teaches discipline, resilience, and respect, helping members grow both inside and outside the ring.

#### Boxing Classes for Every Level and Every Age in Copenhagen

Are you looking for a boxing club for beginners in Copenhagen? AOBC’s classes are designed to build skills and confidence from the ground up. Newcomers are guided through the fundamentals—stance, footwork, and combinations—while experienced boxers get access to advanced training, sparring, and competition prep.

**For youth and kids:** Our youth boxing and kids boxing programs offer a safe, energetic environment for young athletes to develop fitness, coordination, and self-esteem. Searching for “boxing training for kids” or “kids boxing near me” in Copenhagen? AOBC is the answer.

#### Fitness and Wellness Through Boxing in Hellerup and Charlottenlund

Boxing is one of the most effective ways to boost cardiovascular health, strength, and mental focus. Ashira Oure Boxing Club seamlessly blends boxing skills with high-energy fitness workouts—perfect for those interested in boxing fitness or fitness boxing. Our gym with boxing facilities is equipped for group classes, personal coaching, and wellness support.

**Looking for a boxing workout plan, boxing drills, or a boxing training schedule?** Our coaches tailor programs to your ambitions, whether you’re training for your first fight or simply want to get in the best shape of your life.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC offers beginner boxing classes focused on fundamentals like stance, footwork, and combinations. These classes are designed for all ages and skill levels, providing a supportive environment for newcomers to learn boxing basics.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s gyms located in Copenhagen?**  
A2: Ashira Oure Boxing Club has two locations in Copenhagen — one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, both easily accessible for locals seeking top-tier boxing training.

**Q3: Does AOBC provide boxing training programs for kids and youth?**  
A3: Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth boxing and kids boxing programs that focus on fitness, coordination, and discipline in a safe and encouraging environment.

**Q4: Can I get personal coaching or sparring training at AOBC?**  
A4: Absolutely. AOBC provides personal coaching, sparring classes, and competition training tailored to individual goals, whether you are preparing for your first fight or advancing your amateur boxing career.

**Q5: How can I start my boxing journey at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**  
A5: Beginners can book their first boxing class online through the AOBC website. The club welcomes all skill levels and offers tailored programs to meet your fitness and boxing training needs.
