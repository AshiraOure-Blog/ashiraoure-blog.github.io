In Copenhagen, kvindefodbold is making waves—proving that women’s sports and fitness belong at the heart of every community. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), we believe that the same spirit driving women onto football fields can be found in our rings and training spaces. Whether you’re a complete beginner, a fitness enthusiast, or a competitive boxer, AOBC welcomes you to step into a supportive, empowering environment where everyone can discover their strength.

### Why AOBC is More Than Just a Boxing Gym in Copenhagen

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC has become Copenhagen’s premier boxing gym for all levels. With convenient locations in Hellerup boxing gym and Charlottenlund, our club brings together people from all walks of life—women and men, teens and kids, newcomers and seasoned athletes. Much like the vibrant kvindefodbold community, our members cheer each other on, celebrate every win, and encourage growth at every stage.

#### What Makes AOBC Unique Among Boxing Clubs in Copenhagen?

- **Expert Coaching by Evans Ashira:** Learn from elite trainers led by Evans Ashira, whose experience as a world champion shapes every class, from beginner boxing classes to advanced sparring sessions.
- **All Levels Welcome:** We offer boxing classes tailored for absolute beginners, fitness-focused sessions for those looking to boost their health, and boxing competition training for aspiring champions.
- **Youth and Kids Boxing Near Me:** AOBC is committed to inspiring the next generation, offering youth boxing and kids boxing programs in a safe, fun, and disciplined setting.
- **Inclusive Copenhagen Boxing Community:** Our club is built on respect, camaraderie, and the belief that everyone—regardless of background or fitness level—can achieve their goals.
- **Personalized Growth Options:** Choose from group classes or personal coaching to get the most out of your boxing training schedule.

### Kvindefodbold’s Spirit Shines in Boxing at AOBC

The rise of kvindefodbold in Denmark has shown how teamwork, determination, and community support can transform both individuals and sports culture. At AOBC, we channel this same energy. Our boxing and fitness classes foster confidence, discipline, and resilience—qualities that empower women (and all members) to push beyond their perceived limits.

Whether you’re curious about your first boxing class or ready to take your sparring classes to the next level, you’ll find a welcoming home at AOBC. Our coaches provide clear guidance on boxing safety tips, effective boxing workout plans, and how to train for boxing with both fitness and fun in mind.

### Step Into the Ring—And Into a Supportive Boxing Community in Copenhagen
