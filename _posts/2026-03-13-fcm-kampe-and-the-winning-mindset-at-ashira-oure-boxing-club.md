At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), we share this same passion for competition, teamwork, and growth. Whether you’re inspired by the energy of Copenhagen’s sports scene, or searching for a boxing gym that values skill, discipline, and community, AOBC stands as the city’s premier destination for everyone from beginners to competitive fighters.

### Why AOBC is the Boxing Club Copenhagen Trusts

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, Ashira Oure Boxing Club is more than just a place to train—it’s a supportive boxing community where every member is encouraged to reach their full potential. With two convenient locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, AOBC welcomes all ages and skill levels, offering:

- **Expert-led boxing classes:** From beginner boxing classes to advanced sparring and competition prep, our experienced coaches—including Evans Ashira himself—guide you every step of the way.
- **Fitness-focused training:** Our boxing fitness and fitness boxing sessions are tailored to build strength, endurance, and confidence, making AOBC the top boxing gym Copenhagen has to offer.
- **Youth and kids boxing:** With dedicated youth boxing and boxing training for kids, we nurture the next generation of champions in a positive, disciplined environment.
- **Personal coaching:** Whether you dream of stepping into the ring or improving your overall fitness, our coaches work with you individually to help achieve your goals.

### Embracing the Spirit of Competition and Community

Just as fcm kampe unites fans and fuels ambition, AOBC brings together members from all walks of life. Here, beginners are welcomed with open arms, and experienced fighters find the challenge and training they crave. Our focus on discipline, respect, and growth makes AOBC the go-to boxing club for beginners and seasoned athletes alike.

**What makes AOBC unique?**

- **Supportive atmosphere:** Our community is built on encouragement, respect, and inclusivity.
- **Comprehensive programs:** From sparring classes and amateur boxing club sessions to boxing competition training, there’s something for everyone.
- **Safety and skill:** We prioritize boxing safety tips, proper technique, and structured training to ensure a positive experience at every level.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?

AOBC provides beginner boxing classes designed for all fitness levels. These classes focus on fundamentals, boxing drills, safety, and building confidence to ensure a strong foundation for newcomers to the sport.

### Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?

Ashira Oure Boxing Club has two accessible locations in the Copenhagen area: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, making it convenient for locals seeking a boxing gym near them.

### Can kids and youth join boxing training programs at AOBC?

Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth boxing and boxing training for kids, focusing on skill development, fitness, and discipline in a supportive and safe environment.

### Does AOBC provide sparring and competition training?

Absolutely. AOBC includes sparring classes and boxing competition training tailored to prepare amateur boxers for competitive events while emphasizing safety and proper technique.

### How can I join Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s fitness and boxing community?

You can join AOBC by visiting their website or contacting them directly to find out about membership options, class schedules, and personal coaching services designed to suit your goals and experience.
