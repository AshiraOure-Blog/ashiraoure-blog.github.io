## Embracing Every Level of Boxer in Copenhagen

Whether you’re a beginner searching for your first boxing class or an experienced competitor preparing for your next bout, AOBC welcomes you. Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, our club stands out among boxing clubs in Copenhagen for its commitment to fostering both skill and confidence in a supportive atmosphere.

At our two convenient locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, you’ll find:

- **Beginner Boxing Classes:** Perfect for those new to the sport, focused on technique, fitness, and building a foundation.
- **Youth and Kids Boxing:** Our youth boxing and kids boxing near me programs empower the next generation with discipline, fitness, and fun.
- **Sparring and Competition Training:** Tailored sparring classes and boxing competition training for those aiming higher.
- **Personal Coaching:** Individualized plans with expert trainers to help every member—from fitness enthusiasts to amateur boxing club members—reach their goals.

## Fitness, Wellness, and Belonging

The energy that fills grøn koncert 2026 is similar to the buzz inside AOBC. Here, every punch thrown and every drop of sweat contributes to a sense of belonging and accomplishment. Our boxing classes are designed not only for building strength and endurance but also for nurturing mental resilience and wellness—a key value in the Ashira Wellness philosophy.

Whether you want to get fit, learn self-defense, or join a vibrant boxing community, AOBC is the place to start your journey. Our programs are inclusive and designed for all ages and abilities, ensuring that everyone from kids to adults can find their place and thrive.

## What Sets AOBC Apart in Copenhagen

- **Community Spirit:** Like the crowd at grøn koncert, our diverse members support and motivate one another.

- **Comprehensive Training:** From boxing drills and fitness boxing to advanced sparring, our boxing gym in Copenhagen covers it all.
