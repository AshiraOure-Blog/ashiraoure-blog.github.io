#### Champions Inspire Champions

Whether you’re lacing up gloves for your first boxing class or training for your next amateur fight, role models matter. Nikolaj Ehlers, a celebrated athlete with deep Danish roots, reminds us that dedication, hard work, and a supportive community are key to achieving your goals. That’s exactly what you’ll find at Ashira Oure Boxing Club, Copenhagen’s premier destination for boxing and fitness.

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC is more than a boxing gym—it’s a community committed to excellence, growth, and inclusivity. With locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, we offer:

- **Beginner boxing classes** designed for those new to the sport  
- **Youth boxing programs** and kids boxing for families searching for “boxing near me”  
- **Fitness boxing** and group classes for all levels  
- **Personal coaching** to help you unlock your potential  
- **Sparring classes** and competition preparation for aspiring fighters  

#### From Hockey Rinks to Boxing Rings

Just as Ehlers brings his passion and tenacity to every hockey match, AOBC members bring the same spirit to the ring. At AOBC, we believe that every athlete—regardless of their starting point—has the ability to transform. Our coaches, including Evans Ashira, foster skill, discipline, and confidence in every training session. It’s about more than just boxing; it’s about building a mindset for success and well-being.

- **Comprehensive Programs:** We offer everything from fitness workouts to boxing sparring and competition training, so you can find your perfect fit.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC offers beginner boxing classes tailored for newcomers to boxing. These classes focus on foundational skills, fitness, and boxing safety to ensure a supportive start for beginners in Hellerup, Charlottenlund, and greater Copenhagen.

**Q2: Does Ashira Oure Boxing Club provide youth and kids boxing programs?**  
A2: Yes, AOBC has specialized youth boxing and kids boxing programs designed to develop skill, discipline, and fitness for young athletes. These programs are ideal for parents searching for “kids boxing near me” or youth boxing options in Copenhagen.

**Q3: Who founded Ashira Oure Boxing Club and what is their background?**  
A3: Ashira Oure Boxing Club was founded by former world champion boxer Evans Ashira. His expertise and experience shape AOBC’s expert-led training approach, fostering a community focused on skill development, fitness, and competition preparation.

**Q4: What types of sparring and competition training does AOBC offer?**  
A4: AOBC provides sparring classes and boxing competition training for amateur and aspiring fighters. These sessions include coaching on boxing sparring rules, safety tips, and tailored training schedules to prepare members for competitive bouts.

**Q5: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?**  
A5: AOBC operates two main locations in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, making it accessible for residents across the greater Copenhagen area looking for quality boxing gyms.
