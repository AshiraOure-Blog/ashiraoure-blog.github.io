At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, the energy is contagious. When the spotlight from TV2 Nord shines on our passionate boxing community, it’s easy to see why AOBC stands out as Copenhagen’s premier boxing gym. Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, our club in Hellerup and Charlottenlund is more than just a place to train—it’s where beginners, fitness enthusiasts, youth, and competitive athletes find their stride.

#### Why Ashira Oure Boxing Club Leads in Copenhagen

Whether you’re searching for your first boxing class or looking to step up your sparring game, AOBC welcomes every member with open arms. Our mission is simple: to help you become your best, both in and out of the ring.

**What sets Ashira Oure Boxing Club apart?**

- **Expert Coaching**: Train under the guidance of Evans Ashira and our experienced team, who bring world-class knowledge to every session.
- **All Levels Welcome**: AOBC is the boxing club Copenhagen trusts for both beginner boxing classes and advanced competition training.
- **Diverse Programs**: From youth boxing and kids boxing near me, to boxing classes for adults, sparring sessions, and personal coaching—there’s a place for everyone.
- **Supportive Community**: Our members motivate each other, fostering an environment where discipline, respect, and growth are at the core.
- **Fitness and Wellness Focused**: Experience boxing fitness and oure fitness programs designed to boost strength, endurance, and confidence.

#### Boxing for Everyone—From First Timers to Seasoned Fighters

Stepping into a boxing gym for the first time can be intimidating. At Ashira Oure, we make it easy for newcomers to feel at home. Our beginner boxing classes break down the basics, focusing on proper technique, fitness, and safety. If you’re searching for a “boxing club for beginners” or want tips for your first boxing class, our coaches are ready to guide you every step of the way.

For those looking to test their skills, our sparring classes and boxing competition training offer a supportive yet challenging environment. Whether your goal is to compete or simply enjoy the thrill of fitness boxing, AOBC provides the structure, encouragement, and expertise you need.

#### Building the Next Generation of Champions

Youth boxing is a cornerstone of AOBC. We believe in empowering young athletes through discipline, confidence, and community. Our boxing training for kids and youth programs are tailored to develop both skill and character, making AOBC the top choice for parents searching for kids boxing near me in Copenhagen and Hellerup.

#### The AOBC Difference in the Copenhagen Boxing Scene

As a leading copenhagen boxing club (bokseklub københavn), we’re proud of our diverse membership and the spirit that unites us. AOBC stands alongside well-known names like asura boxing club and odyssey boxing club, but our unique focus on personal growth, wellness, and community makes us the gym with boxing that truly feels like home.

#### Ready to Join the AOBC Community?

No matter where you are on your boxing journey, Ashira Oure Boxing Club invites you to experience the difference. Whether you’re aiming for competition, seeking a fun way to get fit, or searching for youth programs, our doors are open.

**Start your journey with Copenhagen’s premier boxing gym—[visit Ashira Oure Boxing Club today](https://www.ashiraoure.com/).**

Find your strength. Find your community. Find yourself at AOBC, where every punch is a step towards your best self.
