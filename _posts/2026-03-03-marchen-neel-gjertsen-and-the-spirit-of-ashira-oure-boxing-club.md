At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, the journey is about more than just punches and footwork—it’s about building confidence, discipline, and a supportive community. Inspired by the tenacity of figures like Marchen Neel Gjertsen, AOBC stands as the premier boxing gym in Copenhagen for everyone, from complete beginners to competitive fighters. Whether you’re eager to try your first boxing class or aiming to compete at the highest level, our club offers expert-led training and a welcoming environment that brings out the best in every member.

## Boxing in Copenhagen for All Levels

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, Ashira Oure Boxing Club has become synonymous with quality, inclusivity, and growth. With locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, AOBC is more than just a **boxing gym in Copenhagen**—it’s a place where you can challenge yourself, meet new friends, and discover your potential.

Our programs are designed to meet every need:

- **Beginner Boxing Classes:** Start your boxing journey with professional guidance, focusing on technique, fitness, and boxing safety tips.  
- **Fitness Boxing:** Combine cardio, strength, and agility in our boxing and fitness classes—perfect for those looking to elevate their workout routine.  
- **Sparring Classes:** Learn the rules and strategies of sparring in a safe, structured setting.  
- **Youth Boxing and Kids’ Programs:** AOBC is a top choice for parents searching for “boxing club for beginners” or “boxing training for kids near me.” Our coaches help youth build self-esteem and discipline.  
- **Competition Training:** For those aiming higher, our seasoned team prepares amateur and competitive boxers for matches in and beyond Copenhagen.

## The AOBC Difference: Community, Expertise, and Growth

What sets AOBC apart from other boxing clubs in Copenhagen—like Richard Olsen Boksning, Asura Boxing Club, or Odyssey Boxing Club—is our unwavering commitment to each member’s progress. 

- **Expert Coaching:** Led by Evans Ashira, our team includes experienced trainers who personalize your boxing workout plan and help you follow a sustainable boxing training schedule.  
- **Supportive Community:** AOBC is a boxing community where encouragement, respect, and camaraderie come first.  
- **Discipline and Wellness:** Boxing isn’t just a sport; it’s a path to better mental and physical health. Our focus on discipline and fitness aligns with the values of Ashira Wellness and Oure Fitness.

## Boxing for Beginners and Competitors Alike

If you’re unsure how to train for boxing, worried about your first boxing class, or seeking advanced boxing drills, AOBC is your home. Our doors are open to all—regardless of age, background, or experience.

- **Youth and Kids’ Boxing:** Parents across Copenhagen trust AOBC for safe, engaging kids’ boxing and youth boxing programs.
- **Women’s Boxing:** We welcome women of all fitness levels, inspired by figures like Marchen Neel Gjertsen, to join our inclusive classes.
- **Competitive Fighters:** Our amateur boxing club nurtures talent and prepares fighters for the ring.

At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, every member is part of a family united by passion, respect, and ambition. If you’re in Copenhagen and want to join a leading boxing club that mirrors the resilience and determination of Marchen Neel Gjertsen, AOBC is the place for you.
