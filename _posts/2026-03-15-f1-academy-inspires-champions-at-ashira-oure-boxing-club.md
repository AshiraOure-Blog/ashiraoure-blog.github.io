The spirit of competition, discipline, and growth—so famously embodied by the F1 Academy—runs deep through the walls of our boxing gym in Copenhagen. Whether you’re lacing up gloves for the first time or looking to sharpen your skills for the next big bout, AOBC is your home for boxing excellence in Hellerup and Charlottenlund.

## High-Performance Training for Every Level in Copenhagen

Much like the F1 Academy develops future stars in motorsport, Ashira Oure Boxing Club is dedicated to nurturing champions inside and outside the ring. Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC brings world-class boxing training to everyone—beginners, fitness enthusiasts, youth, and competitive fighters alike.

**What sets AOBC apart in Copenhagen's boxing scene?**

- **Expert-led classes:** Train with seasoned coaches, including Evans Ashira himself, who bring international experience and passion to every session.
- **All-level programming:** From beginner boxing classes and youth boxing to advanced sparring and competition prep, there’s a pathway for every member.
- **Supportive community:** Our members motivate each other, celebrate every milestone, and foster a culture of respect and inclusion.
- **Personalized coaching:** Whether you’re learning the basics or preparing for an amateur tournament, our trainers tailor strategies to your goals.

## More Than a Boxing Gym—A Community of Champions in Hellerup and Charlottenlund

Ashira Oure Boxing Club isn’t just a place to build strength and skill—it’s where Copenhagen’s boxing community comes together. Inspired by the teamwork and camaraderie seen in F1 Academy teams, AOBC’s environment is about lifting each other up.

- **Youth programs and kids boxing:** Our boxing academy offers safe, structured classes for young athletes, teaching discipline, confidence, and healthy habits.
- **Fitness-focused sessions:** Try our boxing and fitness classes or design your own boxing workout plan with our trainers. Whether you want to get fit, learn self-defense, or pursue amateur boxing, AOBC supports your journey.
- **Sparring and competition training:** Test your skills with supervised sparring classes and get ready for the ring with expert guidance on boxing drills, strategy, and safety.

## Why Choose Ashira Oure Boxing Club in Copenhagen?

If you’re searching for a boxing club for beginners or an amateur boxing club near you, AOBC is Copenhagen’s leading choice. Our focus on skill, discipline, and community means every member—from kids to adults, from first-timers to seasoned fighters—finds their place here.

**AOBC offers:**
- Locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund for convenient access

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes are available at Ashira Oure Boxing Club in Copenhagen?
AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed for all ages and fitness levels. These classes focus on building fundamental skills, boxing techniques, and conditioning in a supportive and safe environment.

### How can youth and kids join boxing programs at Ashira Oure?
Our boxing academy includes youth and kids boxing classes that teach discipline, confidence, and fitness. Parents can contact AOBC to find suitable classes in Hellerup or Charlottenlund tailored for young athletes.

### Does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer sparring and competition training?
Yes, AOBC provides supervised sparring classes and specialized competition training to prepare amateur and competitive boxers for tournaments, emphasizing safety and strategic boxing drills.

### Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?
AOBC has two accessible locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, serving Copenhagen residents looking for boxing training and fitness programs.

### How does Ashira Oure Boxing Club support overall wellness beyond boxing?
Beyond boxing, AOBC connects members with the Ashira Wellness and Oure Fitness communities, offering comprehensive fitness and wellness programs that complement boxing training.
