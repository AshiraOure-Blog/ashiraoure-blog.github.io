## Start Strong at AOBC: Tips for Beginners

New to boxing? AOBC is the ultimate **amateur boxing club** for beginners. Our team believes in breaking down barriers and making everyone feel welcome from day one. Here’s how to make the most of your first sessions:

- **Embrace the Basics:** Mastering stance, footwork, and guard is the foundation. Our **beginner boxing classes** are designed to build your confidence and form.
- **Ask Questions:** AOBC’s coaches, including Evans Ashira himself, are approachable and ready to help you understand every drill.
- **Prioritize Safety:** Learn key **boxing safety tips** in each class—safe training is smart training.
- **Stay Consistent:** Progress comes with regular attendance. Check out our [boxing training schedule](https://www.ashiraoure.com/) and set a routine.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What makes Ashira Oure Boxing Club the best boxing gym in Copenhagen?
Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers expert coaching led by former world champion Evans Ashira, top-tier facilities in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, and a supportive community for all levels—from beginners to competitive boxers.

### Do you offer beginner boxing classes for those new to the sport?
Yes, AOBC specializes in beginner boxing classes designed to teach foundational skills such as stance, footwork, and guard, with a strong emphasis on safety and gradual progression.

### Are there boxing programs available for kids and youth in Copenhagen?
Absolutely. AOBC offers dedicated youth boxing and boxing training for kids programs that focus on skill development, discipline, and fun in a safe, community-oriented environment.

### How can I prepare for boxing competitions at AOBC?
