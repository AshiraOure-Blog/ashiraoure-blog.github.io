As the nytår arrives in Copenhagen, it’s the perfect moment to embrace new challenges, set ambitious goals, and invest in yourself. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, we believe that every fresh start is an opportunity to become stronger—physically, mentally, and as part of a supportive community.

## Why AOBC Is Copenhagen’s Boxing Destination

Whether you’re just starting your fitness journey, seeking a motivating boxing class, or preparing for competition, AOBC is your home for growth. With locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, our boxing club welcomes everyone—from complete beginners to experienced fighters. Here’s what sets AOBC apart:

- **Expert Guidance:** Train with Evans Ashira and a team of passionate coaches, each dedicated to teaching the art of boxing with skill, patience, and encouragement.
- **Inclusive Community:** At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, everyone belongs—youth, adults, and families. Our members support each other inside and outside the ring.
- **Diverse Offerings:** We provide beginner boxing classes, youth boxing, sparring classes, competition prep, personal coaching, and fitness boxing workouts.
- **Holistic Focus:** AOBC is about more than punches and footwork; we build discipline, resilience, and confidence for life.

## Kickstart Your Nytår With Boxing

Thinking about your nytår goals? Boxing is an effective way to boost your fitness, challenge yourself, and gain new skills. Here’s how AOBC helps you start strong:

### For Beginners

- **Welcoming Environment:** Our coaches guide you through your first boxing class, ensuring you learn at your own pace in a safe, encouraging space.
- **Skill Building:** From basic footwork to bag work and boxing drills, you’ll develop technique and confidence.
- **Fitness Benefits:** Boxing training is a full-body workout, improving strength, stamina, and agility.

### For Youth and Families

- **Youth Boxing Programs:** AOBC’s youth boxing and kids boxing classes introduce children to the sport in a fun, structured way, emphasizing fitness and teamwork.
- **Safety First:** We prioritize boxing safety tips and proper technique, creating a secure place for young athletes to thrive.

### For Competitors

- **Advanced Coaching:** Work with Evans Ashira and our experienced trainers to hone your skills, master sparring, and prepare for competition.
- **Comprehensive Training:** Access tailored boxing workout plans and boxing training schedules that push you to achieve your best.

## Join the AOBC Community

At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, every member is part of a larger family—connected by a shared commitment to growth, discipline, and mutual respect. Whether you’re in Hellerup, Charlottenlund, or anywhere in Copenhagen, AOBC is your boxing community.

Take the next step this nytår. Discover boxing classes, personal coaching, youth programs, and more at [Ashira Oure Boxing Club](https://www.ashiraoure.com/). Unleash your potential and start your journey with AOBC—where every punch brings you closer to your goals.

Ready to join Copenhagen’s leading boxing club? See our class schedules and sign up today at [ashiraoure.com](https://www.ashiraoure.com/). Your strongest year begins now—together at AOBC.
