When UFC fever sweeps through Copenhagen, it ignites a passion for combat sports in people of all ages and backgrounds. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), we channel that same energy, drive, and discipline into every training session. Whether you dream of stepping into the ring or want to elevate your fitness routine, AOBC—founded by world champion Evans Ashira—offers the perfect environment to grow, challenge yourself, and join a supportive boxing community.

### The UFC Spirit Meets Boxing Excellence in Copenhagen

The UFC is renowned for its intensity, athleticism, and diversity of fighting styles. At AOBC, we embrace these values, providing a home for everyone from complete beginners to seasoned competitors. Our boxing gym in Copenhagen, with convenient locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, is more than just a place to work out—it’s a place to belong.

#### What Sets Ashira Oure Boxing Club Apart?

- **All Levels Welcome:** From your first jab to advanced sparring, AOBC’s classes are tailored to suit every experience level. Our beginner boxing classes and boxing club for beginners provide a safe, encouraging start.
- **Expert Coaching:** Train with Evans Ashira and a team of passionate coaches. Benefit from world-class instruction, whether you’re interested in boxing fitness, youth boxing, or competition prep.
- **Comprehensive Programs:** Explore everything from fitness boxing and boxing workout plans to youth and kids boxing near you. AOBC offers specialized boxing training for kids and adults alike.
- **Community and Support:** Step into a welcoming community that values respect, discipline, and personal growth. Build lifelong friendships and train in an environment where everyone lifts each other up.
- **Safety First:** Our coaches focus on boxing safety tips, proper technique, and injury prevention, so you can train with confidence—whether you’re sparring or hitting the heavy bag.

### For Beginners and Champions Alike

Inspired by the UFC’s commitment to athlete development, AOBC is the leading boxing club in Copenhagen for anyone seeking transformative results. New to boxing? Our first boxing class is designed to make you feel comfortable and motivated. Eager to compete? Our boxing competition training and sparring classes will challenge and prepare you for the next level.

Our youth boxing academy helps young athletes build confidence, discipline, and fitness in a fun, structured setting. For adults, AOBC’s boxing and fitness classes are the perfect way to boost health, reduce stress, and unleash your inner warrior.

### Why Choose AOBC Over Other Boxing Gyms?

- **Founded by a World Champion:** Evans Ashira’s legacy and passion infuse every session at AOBC.
- **Accessible Locations:** Visit us in Hellerup or Charlottenlund—ideal for those searching for a Copenhagen boxing club.
- **Inclusive Philosophy:** We believe everyone—regardless of age, background, or ability—can benefit from boxing’s physical and mental rewards.
- **Personalized Attention:** Whether you want to join group classes or work with a personal coach, our team supports your goals every step of the way.

### Ready to Experience the UFC Mindset at AOBC?

If you’re inspired by the UFC and want to experience the thrill, discipline, and camaraderie of expert-led boxing, Ashira Oure Boxing Club is your destination. Join our vibrant boxing community in Copenhagen and start your journey—whether for fitness, fun, or the competitive edge.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC provides beginner boxing classes designed for people new to boxing, focusing on foundational skills, fitness, and safety. Classes are held at both Hellerup and Charlottenlund locations, ideal for anyone looking to start boxing in Copenhagen.

**Q2: How can I prepare for boxing competitions at AOBC?**  
A2: AOBC offers specialized boxing competition training and sparring classes led by expert coaches, including world champion Evans Ashira. These programs focus on technique, strategy, fitness, and mental preparation to help you succeed in amateur boxing competitions.

**Q3: Does AOBC have programs for youth and kids interested in boxing?**  
A3: Yes, AOBC runs a youth boxing academy and offers boxing training for kids, providing a fun, safe, and structured environment to develop skills, confidence, and fitness for young athletes.

**Q4: Where are the Ashira Oure Boxing Club gyms located in Copenhagen?**  
A4: AOBC operates two convenient gyms in the Copenhagen area: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, making it accessible for residents searching for a boxing gym in these neighborhoods.

**Q5: What makes Ashira Oure Boxing Club different from other boxing gyms in Copenhagen?**  
A5: AOBC combines world-class coaching by former world champion Evans Ashira, inclusive programs for all skill levels, a strong community focus, and a commitment to safety and personal growth, making it the premier boxing club in Copenhagen.
