Whether you’re a newcomer taking your first steps into the ring or a seasoned athlete aiming for your next title, AOBC is where every journey is celebrated and every member is championed.

### Building Champions in Copenhagen at Ashira Oure Boxing Club

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC is more than just a boxing gym in Copenhagen. Our locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund are hubs where skill, fitness, and confidence are built through expert-led classes and a supportive community. We offer:

- **Beginner boxing classes** tailored for those new to the sport, with a focus on fundamental techniques and boxing safety tips.
- **Fitness boxing and sparring classes** designed to boost your cardiovascular strength and sharpen your skills.
- **Personal coaching** for those seeking one-on-one guidance and a personalized boxing workout plan.
- **Youth and kids boxing programs**, providing a safe and motivating environment for young athletes to grow, learn discipline, and build self-esteem.
- **Boxing competition training** for amateurs and competitive fighters determined to reach their full potential.

### The Power of Community and Discipline at AOBC Copenhagen

The Ashira Oure Boxing Club difference lies in our inclusive and inspiring approach. Our members—ranging from complete beginners to experienced competitors—support each other through every punch, drill, and challenge. AOBC’s community is bound by a shared commitment to personal growth, much like the dedication and poise Dronning Mary brings to Denmark’s cultural life.

We believe that boxing is more than a sport; it’s a journey of self-discovery and transformation. Our coaches, including Evans Ashira, Lucas Ashira, and Mino Ashira, bring a wealth of knowledge and motivation to every session. With a focus on both skill and fitness, AOBC is the boxing club Copenhagen trusts for results that go beyond the ring.
