## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed for those new to boxing, focusing on fundamental skills, safety, and building confidence in a supportive environment. These classes are available at both Hellerup and Charlottenlund locations.

**Q2: Where is Ashira Oure Boxing Club located in Copenhagen?**  
A2: Ashira Oure Boxing Club has two convenient locations in Copenhagen: Hellerup and Charlottenlund, making it accessible for residents across the city and surrounding areas.

**Q3: Can kids join boxing training at AOBC?**  
A3: Yes, AOBC provides specialized youth boxing programs and kids boxing classes tailored to develop skills, fitness, and discipline for young athletes in a safe and encouraging atmosphere.

**Q4: Does AOBC offer sparring and competition training?**  
A4: Absolutely. AOBC offers sparring classes and boxing competition training designed to prepare amateur and competitive boxers with expert coaching and adherence to boxing sparring rules.

**Q5: How can I start my boxing fitness journey with Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**
