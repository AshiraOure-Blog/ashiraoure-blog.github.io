- **Youth and Kids Programs:** Our boxing academy provides safe, structured boxing training for kids and teens, fostering discipline, fitness, and self-confidence.
- **Community Spirit:** AOBC is more than a boxing gym in Copenhagen—it’s a place where friendships are forged, and everyone pushes each other to new heights.
- **Flexible Training:** Whether you’re interested in fitness boxing, sparring classes, competition prep, or personal coaching, you’ll find a program that fits your needs.

## Building Strength and Resilience

Just as snefygning tests Copenhagen’s endurance, boxing tests your own. Our members benefit from:

- **Structured Boxing Workout Plans:** Tailored to your fitness level and goals.
- **Safety-First Approach:** We teach boxing safety tips and sparring rules, ensuring a safe and fun environment.
- **Discipline and Focus:** Boxing isn’t just about strength—it’s about building mental resilience, focus, and a sense of achievement.
- **Year-Round Training:** No need to pause your progress when winter hits. Our Hellerup and Charlottenlund locations are ready for you, whatever the weather.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?  
AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed to teach fundamental boxing techniques, fitness boxing, and build confidence for new boxers in a supportive environment.

### Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?  
AOBC operates two main locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, providing accessible boxing training options across the Copenhagen area.

### Can kids and youth join boxing training at AOBC?  
Yes, AOBC has specialized youth and kids boxing programs focused on discipline, fitness, and safety to help young aspiring boxers develop their skills.

### Who founded Ashira Oure Boxing Club?  
The club was founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, who brings professional experience and passion to coaching all levels of boxers.

### Does AOBC offer sparring and competition training?  
Yes, AOBC provides sparring classes and boxing competition training tailored to prepare boxers for amateur and professional bouts.
