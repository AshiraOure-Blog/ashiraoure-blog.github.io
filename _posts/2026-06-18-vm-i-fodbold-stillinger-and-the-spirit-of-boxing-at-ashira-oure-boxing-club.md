## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners?  
Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers beginner boxing classes designed to teach fundamental techniques in a supportive and welcoming environment. These classes focus on building skills, fitness, and confidence for those new to boxing.

### Where are AOBC’s boxing gyms located in Copenhagen?  
AOBC has two main locations in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, serving the greater Copenhagen area with top-tier boxing training and facilities.

### Can children join boxing training at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?  
Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth boxing and kids boxing programs that focus on discipline, safety, and fun to help young boxers develop skills and confidence.
