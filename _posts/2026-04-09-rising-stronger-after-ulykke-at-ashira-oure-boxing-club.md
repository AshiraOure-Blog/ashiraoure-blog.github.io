Unexpected challenges—known in Danish as *ulykke*—can strike anyone, whether you’re stepping into the ring for the first time or preparing for your next boxing competition. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), founded by world champion Evans Ashira, we believe that setbacks are not the end of the story. They are the beginning of your comeback.

### Embracing Ulykke in Your Boxing Journey

Every boxer, from absolute beginners to seasoned champions, encounters obstacles. Maybe you sprained a wrist during sparring, or perhaps your first boxing class didn’t go as planned. Ulykke, or misfortune, is part of every athlete’s path. What matters most is how you respond.

At AOBC, we turn challenges into motivation. Our expert trainers—including Evans Ashira himself—know firsthand how to recover, rebuild, and emerge stronger. Whether you train at our Hellerup or Charlottenlund locations, you’ll discover a supportive community ready to lift you up when you need it most.

### Safety and Support at Copenhagen’s Premier Boxing Club

Your safety is our top priority. AOBC leads the way among boxing clubs in Copenhagen by providing:

- **Structured beginner boxing classes** with safety tips and clear guidance
- **Personal coaching** to tailor your boxing workout plan and recovery strategies
- **Boxing fitness programs** that focus on skill, strength, and injury prevention
- **Sparring classes** with strict adherence to boxing sparring rules for safe practice
- **Youth boxing and kids boxing** sessions led by experienced coaches who nurture confidence and discipline

Our trainers are committed to teaching boxing safety tips and helping you avoid common pitfalls. If you ever experience an ulykke, our team will guide you every step of the way—so you can get back to doing what you love.

### Turning Setbacks Into Success

At AOBC, ulykke isn’t just a hurdle—it’s an opportunity to grow. Our community in Copenhagen is built on resilience, camaraderie, and the shared pursuit of wellness. We encourage every member, from kids and teens to adults and competitive boxers, to:

- **Learn proper technique** to minimize injury risk
- **Ask for help**—our trainers and fellow members are here for you
- **Stay committed** to your boxing training schedule, even if you need to adjust your pace
- **Celebrate your progress**—every small win counts

### Why AOBC Is the Right Boxing Club for You

Choosing the right boxing gym in Copenhagen means finding a place where you feel safe, supported, and inspired. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, you’ll find:

- Expert coaching from Evans Ashira and his dedicated team
- A focus on both fitness and boxing skills
- Classes for all levels—including absolute beginners and youth programs
- A community atmosphere where every setback fuels your comeback

Whether you’re looking for your first boxing class, seeking a gym with boxing and fitness classes, or preparing for amateur boxing competitions, AOBC is here to help you rise above every ulykke.

### Join the AOBC Community Today

Ready to turn setbacks into strength? Join Ashira Oure Boxing Club—the Copenhagen boxing club where every member is empowered to grow, no matter what challenges come their way. [Book your first class or learn more about our programs here.](https://www.ashiraoure.com/)

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC offers structured beginner boxing classes tailored for all skill levels, focusing on fundamentals, boxing safety tips, and fitness boxing to help newcomers build confidence and technique.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s training locations in Copenhagen?**  
A2: AOBC has two main locations for boxing training in Copenhagen: the Hellerup boxing gym and the Charlottenlund facility, both equipped for fitness boxing, sparring classes, and youth boxing programs.

**Q3: How does AOBC support recovery after an injury or ulykke?**  
A3: The club provides personal coaching and tailored boxing workout plans that include recovery strategies, boxing safety tips, and expert guidance from Evans Ashira and his team to ensure safe and effective healing.

**Q4: Does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer boxing training for kids and youth?**  
A4: Yes, AOBC has dedicated youth boxing and kids boxing sessions led by experienced coaches who focus on building discipline, confidence, and proper technique in a safe environment.

**Q5: Can beginners join sparring classes at AOBC?**  
A5: Absolutely. AOBC offers sparring classes with strict adherence to boxing sparring rules, ensuring a safe and supportive experience for beginners and advanced boxers alike.
