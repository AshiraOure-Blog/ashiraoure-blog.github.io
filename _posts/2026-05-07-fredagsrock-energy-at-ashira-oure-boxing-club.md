Friday evenings in Copenhagen take on a special energy, thanks to the city’s popular fredagsrock tradition—a time when people come together to celebrate, recharge, and enjoy community. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), we channel that same spirit every week, transforming our boxing gym in Hellerup and Charlottenlund into a vibrant hub of fitness, skill, and camaraderie. Whether you’re a beginner looking for your first boxing class, a youth athlete eager to learn, or a competitive boxer sharpening your edge, AOBC is the perfect place to kick off your weekend with purpose.

## Experience the AOBC Difference in Copenhagen

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC stands out as Copenhagen’s premier boxing club. Our mission is simple: empower every member, regardless of experience or background, to reach their full potential. Our expert coaches—including Evans Ashira himself—lead a variety of classes designed to spark your passion for boxing while building fitness, discipline, and confidence.

### What Makes AOBC the Ultimate Boxing Community in Copenhagen?

- **All Levels Welcome**: From complete beginners to seasoned fighters, our classes are tailored to your needs. If you’ve ever searched for a boxing club for beginners or wondered about your first boxing class, AOBC is your home.
- **Holistic Training**: We offer more than just traditional boxing. Try fitness boxing, join sparring classes, or embark on a personalized boxing workout plan with our trainers.
- **Youth and Kids Programs**: Our youth boxing and boxing training for kids nurture discipline and athleticism in a supportive, fun environment. Looking for “kids boxing near me” in Copenhagen? Join our growing AOBC family.
- **Competition Prep**: Ambitious about stepping into the ring? Take part in our boxing competition training and amateur boxing club sessions to prepare for your next challenge.
- **Supportive Community**: AOBC isn’t just a gym—it’s a boxing community. Members motivate each other and celebrate every achievement, big or small.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?
Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC) offers beginner boxing classes tailored for all ages and fitness levels, providing foundational skills, safety training, and fitness boxing to help newcomers start their boxing journey confidently in Copenhagen.

### Where are the Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?
AOBC has two convenient locations in Copenhagen: Hellerup and Charlottenlund. Both gyms provide comprehensive boxing training, youth programs, and sparring classes ideal for anyone searching for a boxing gym in Copenhagen.

### Can kids join boxing training programs at AOBC?
Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth and kids boxing training programs designed to develop athleticism, discipline, and confidence in a safe and supportive environment. It’s perfect for parents looking for “kids boxing near me” in Copenhagen.

### How does AOBC support competitive boxing training in Copenhagen?
AOBC provides expert-led boxing competition training and amateur boxing club sessions, preparing athletes with advanced boxing drills, sparring classes, and personalized workout plans to excel in competitions.

### Is there a community aspect at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?
Absolutely! AOBC prides itself on fostering a supportive boxing community where members motivate each other, celebrate progress, and build friendships, making it more than just a gym in Copenhagen.
