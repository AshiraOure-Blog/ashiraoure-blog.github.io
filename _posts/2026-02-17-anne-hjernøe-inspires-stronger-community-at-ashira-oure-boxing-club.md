At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), inspiration comes from all corners of Copenhagen—whether it’s a new member lacing up their gloves for the first time, or a well-known personality like Anne Hjernøe who reminds us of the power of community and well-being. AOBC, founded by world champion Evans Ashira, is more than just a boxing club in Copenhagen. It’s a thriving community where beginners, fitness enthusiasts, youth, and competitive athletes come together to build strength, confidence, and resilience.

## Embracing New Challenges at AOBC in Copenhagen, Denmark

Just as Anne Hjernøe has inspired many to step into new adventures, AOBC invites everyone—regardless of background or experience—to take on the challenge of boxing training. With locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, AOBC is the leading boxing gym Copenhagen residents trust for skill development, fitness, and personal growth.

### Why Choose Ashira Oure Boxing Club in Copenhagen?

- **Expert-Led Classes:** Led by Evans Ashira and passionate coaches, our classes cater to all levels. From beginner boxing classes and youth boxing to advanced sparring and competition training, there’s something for everyone.
- **Supportive Community:** Like Anne Hjernøe’s commitment to inclusivity and well-being, AOBC fosters a positive, encouraging environment. Members motivate each other, celebrate success, and grow stronger together.
- **Focus on Fitness and Wellness:** AOBC combines boxing fitness with holistic health. Our boxing workout plans, fitness boxing sessions, and personal coaching help you reach your goals, whether you’re just getting started or preparing for competitions.
- **Youth and Family Programs:** Looking for kids boxing near me? Our youth boxing and boxing training for kids programs focus on discipline, fun, and fitness, making AOBC a favorite among families across Copenhagen.

## Boxing for Every Journey at AOBC Copenhagen

Whether you’re searching for a bokseklub København, a Hellerup boxing gym, or a boxing club for beginners, AOBC welcomes you. Our trainers, including Evans Ashira, Yuna Ashira, Lucas Ashira, and Mino Ashira, bring a wealth of experience and passion. We offer:

- **Boxing and fitness classes** for all ages and abilities
- **Personal training** tailored to your needs
- **Sparring classes** and competition prep for amateur and seasoned boxers
- **Boxing academy programs** designed to nurture talent and foster discipline

## Inspired by Community, Driven by Results at AOBC

Like Anne Hjernøe’s approach to health and community, AOBC believes that everyone can achieve their best with the right support. Our boxing community is built on trust, respect, and a shared passion for growth. Whether you want to get fit, gain confidence, or compete, AOBC is the place to start your journey.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners?**  
AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed to teach fundamental skills, boxing drills, and fitness boxing techniques in a supportive environment ideal for newcomers.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?**  
AOBC has two convenient locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, serving the greater Copenhagen area with expert coaching and community-driven boxing programs.

**Q3: Does AOBC provide youth and kids boxing programs?**  
Yes, Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers specialized youth boxing and boxing training for kids, focusing on discipline, fitness, and fun to engage young athletes safely.

**Q4: Can I prepare for competitions at AOBC?**  
Absolutely. AOBC provides sparring classes and boxing competition training guided by experienced coaches including former world champion Evans Ashira to help you prepare effectively.

**Q5: How can I start my first boxing class at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**  
You can join your first boxing class by visiting the AOBC website and signing up. The club welcomes beginners and provides a friendly introduction to boxing and fitness.
