## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?
Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers beginner boxing classes designed for all ages and skill levels, focusing on foundational techniques, fitness, and safety. These classes are perfect for those new to boxing looking to build skills in a supportive environment.

### How can youth and kids join boxing training at AOBC in Copenhagen?
AOBC provides specialized youth boxing programs and boxing training for kids that emphasize skill development, discipline, and confidence-building. Parents can enroll their children through the club’s website or by visiting one of the Copenhagen locations.

### What makes Ashira Oure Boxing Club the best boxing gym in Hellerup?

### Does AOBC offer sparring and competition training for amateur boxers?

Absolutely. AOBC provides personal coaching sessions to help members refine boxing techniques, improve fitness levels, and reach their individual goals, whether for fitness, competition, or personal development.
