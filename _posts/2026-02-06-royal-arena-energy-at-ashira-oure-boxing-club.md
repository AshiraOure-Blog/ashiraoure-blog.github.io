When the buzz of major events fills Royal Arena, you can feel the pulse of Copenhagen’s athletic spirit. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), that same energy fuels every training session, sparring match, and fitness class. Whether you’re a boxing beginner, a youth athlete, or a seasoned competitor, AOBC channels the excitement and ambition of Royal Arena into a daily commitment to growth, skill, and community.

## Experience the AOBC Difference in Copenhagen

Founded by former world champion **Evans Ashira**, AOBC stands out as the premier **boxing gym in Copenhagen**. With top-tier facilities in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, our club brings together people from all backgrounds to learn, push limits, and find their fighting spirit. Just as Royal Arena draws crowds for unforgettable moments, AOBC brings together a supportive **boxing community** where every member is encouraged to strive for their best.

### What Makes AOBC Special?

- **Expert Coaching for All Levels:**  
  From your **first boxing class** to advanced **boxing competition training**, our coaches—including Evans Ashira himself—guide you every step of the way. Whether you're curious about **boxing classes for beginners** or ready to elevate your amateur boxing journey, AOBC offers tailored programs for everyone.

- **Comprehensive Training Options:**  
  Our schedule features **fitness boxing**, technical drills, sparring sessions, and **boxing workout plans**. Youth and kids are welcome through our **youth boxing** and **boxing training for kids** programs, fostering discipline and confidence from an early age.

- **A Welcoming Boxing Community:**  
  At AOBC, we believe boxing is for everyone. Our members include fitness enthusiasts, youth, and competitive athletes, all united by their passion for growth. The supportive atmosphere ensures you’ll find encouragement, motivation, and friendship inside—and outside—the ring.

- **Focus on Safety and Wellness:**  
  As part of the broader **ashira wellness** philosophy, our trainers emphasize proper technique, safety, and personal progress. You’ll receive guidance on **boxing sparring rules**, injury prevention, and healthy training routines, so you can train smart and stay strong.

## From Inspiration to Action in Hellerup and Charlottenlund

Royal Arena inspires with its grand stage and unforgettable moments. At AOBC, we create those moments every day—through hard work, determination, and community spirit. Whether you’re preparing for your first sparring session or eyeing a competition, our expert team will help you build the skills, fitness, and discipline to shine.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?  
AOBC provides beginner boxing classes designed to teach fundamental skills, fitness, and discipline in a supportive environment. These classes are perfect for those new to boxing and looking to get started safely.

### Where are the Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?  
AOBC has two main locations: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, both easily accessible for residents of Copenhagen and nearby areas.

### How can youth and kids benefit from AOBC’s boxing training programs?  
Our youth boxing and boxing training for kids programs focus on building confidence, discipline, and fitness through age-appropriate training and expert coaching.

### Does AOBC offer sparring and competition training for amateur boxers?  
Yes, AOBC provides sparring classes and boxing competition training tailored to help amateur boxers prepare for matches with proper technique and safety.

### What makes AOBC different from other boxing gyms in Copenhagen?  
Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC combines expert coaching, comprehensive training options, a welcoming boxing community, and a strong focus on wellness and safety, making it a top choice for all boxing levels.
