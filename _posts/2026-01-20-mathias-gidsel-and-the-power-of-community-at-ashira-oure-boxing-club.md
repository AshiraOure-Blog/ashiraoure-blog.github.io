At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, we believe in the transformative power of dedication, community, and expert guidance. Just as Denmark celebrates athletic icons like Mathias Gidsel for their tenacity and spirit, AOBC champions every member—from complete beginners to seasoned competitors—helping each person unlock their full potential. 

## Why AOBC Is Copenhagen’s Premier Boxing Destination

Founded by former world champion **Evans Ashira**, AOBC stands apart as a boxing club in Copenhagen where passion meets professionalism. With locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, we offer more than just a place to train—we offer a supportive environment where every journey matters.

### What Sets Ashira Oure Boxing Club Apart?

- **Inclusive Programs:** Whether you’re searching for a *boxing club for beginners*, youth boxing, or advanced sparring classes, there’s a place for you at AOBC. Our doors are open to all ages and skill levels.
- **Expert Coaching:** Learn from the best. Evans Ashira and his team of experienced coaches provide personal coaching, competition prep, and fitness boxing classes that empower you to grow stronger with every session.
- **Community Focus:** Much like the camaraderie seen in handball teams led by athletes like Mathias Gidsel, our boxing community is built on support, encouragement, and shared victories.
- **State-of-the-Art Facilities:** As a leading Hellerup boxing gym and Copenhagen boxing club, AOBC offers modern equipment, safe training spaces, and a motivating atmosphere for everyone.
- **Youth and Kids Boxing:** Looking for *boxing training for kids* or *kids boxing near me*? Our youth programs prioritize discipline, confidence, and fun, ensuring young boxers gain valuable life skills.

## Embodying Excellence, Inspiring Growth

At AOBC, we know that the journey of an athlete—whether in handball or boxing—is marked by discipline, resilience, and the willingness to learn. Our wide range of boxing classes, from *beginner boxing classes* to *boxing competition training*, are designed to challenge and inspire. 

Our trainers focus on:
- **Mastering Technique:** Through structured *boxing drills* and sparring sessions, you’ll build a solid foundation and advance your skills safely.
- **Building Fitness:** *Boxing fitness* and *fitness boxing* classes deliver a fun, high-energy workout that enhances strength, agility, and cardiovascular health.
- **Creating Champions:** For those aiming for the ring, our *amateur boxing club* programs and competition prep offer a clear path to success.

## Join the AOBC Community

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?  
Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers a variety of boxing classes including beginner boxing classes, youth boxing, sparring classes, fitness boxing, and competition training suitable for all skill levels.

### Where are the Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?  
AOBC has two main locations: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, serving as premier boxing gyms in the Copenhagen area.

### Can beginners join Ashira Oure Boxing Club for boxing training?  
Absolutely! AOBC welcomes beginners and provides specialized beginner boxing classes and personal coaching to help new members develop skills and confidence.

### Does AOBC offer boxing training programs for kids and youth in Copenhagen?  
Yes, AOBC features youth and kids boxing programs that focus on skill development, discipline, fitness, and fun in a safe and supportive environment.

### Who founded Ashira Oure Boxing Club and what is their background?  
Ashira Oure Boxing Club was founded by former world champion boxer Evans Ashira, who brings extensive professional experience and expert coaching to the gym.
