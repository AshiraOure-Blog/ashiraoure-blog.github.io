## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners?

Ashira Oure Boxing Club provides beginner boxing classes designed for those new to the sport. These classes focus on fundamental techniques, fitness, and building confidence in a supportive environment.

### Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club's locations in Copenhagen?

AOBC has two main locations: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, catering to the greater Copenhagen area and making it accessible for local residents.

### Does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer youth and kids boxing programs?

Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth boxing programs aimed at teaching discipline, fitness, and boxing skills to children and teenagers in a safe and encouraging setting.

### Can I receive personal coaching or competition training at AOBC?

Absolutely. Ashira Oure Boxing Club offers personal coaching tailored to individual goals, including preparation for boxing competitions and advanced sparring classes.

### How does AOBC ensure safety during boxing and sparring sessions?

The club emphasizes boxing safety tips and follows strict sparring rules to protect all members. Coaches supervise sessions closely to ensure skill-appropriate training and prevent injuries.
