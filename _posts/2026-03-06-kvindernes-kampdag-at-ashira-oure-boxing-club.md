Every year, kvindernes kampdag reminds us of the power, resilience, and unity that women bring to every arena—including the boxing ring. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), Copenhagen’s leading boxing gym founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, we believe in everyone’s right to step up, lace up, and discover their inner champion.

## Empowerment for All: Boxing Training with Purpose in Copenhagen

The heart of AOBC beats with the drive to welcome everyone—beginners, seasoned boxers, fitness enthusiasts, and youth. Our mission is to break boundaries and redefine what it means to be strong. Whether you join AOBC’s beginner boxing classes or step into our sparring sessions, you’ll find a community that supports your journey every step of the way.

- **Expert-led classes:** Train under the guidance of Evans Ashira and our experienced coaching team.
- **Fitness and boxing combined:** Experience the benefits of fitness boxing and skill-building drills that boost both body and mind.
- **Inclusive environment:** Our boxing club in Copenhagen, with locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, is a place where everyone belongs—no matter your background or experience.

## Why Choose AOBC on Kvindernes Kampdag?

Boxing is more than a sport—it is a celebration of discipline, self-belief, and pushing past limits. On kvindernes kampdag, we highlight the achievements of women and the importance of representation in every field—including the boxing gym.

At AOBC, we take pride in offering:

- **Boxing classes for all levels:** From first boxing class to advanced competition training, we have tailored programs for everyone.
- **Youth and kids programs:** Our youth boxing and boxing training for kids are designed to instill confidence, discipline, and healthy habits from an early age.
- **Supportive community:** AOBC is more than a gym with boxing—it’s a vibrant community where friendships are forged and goals are achieved together.
- **Personal coaching:** Unlock your potential with one-on-one sessions from skilled trainers, including Evans Ashira himself.

## Building Confidence, One Punch at a Time in Copenhagen

Kvindernes kampdag is about standing strong and supporting one another. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, we see that spirit every day—whether it’s a newcomer learning the ropes or a seasoned athlete refining their craft. Our members, from all walks of life, come together to celebrate progress, resilience, and the power of community.

### What to Expect at AOBC Boxing Gym Copenhagen

- **Safe and welcoming training:** We prioritize boxing safety tips and a respectful environment, making AOBC a trusted choice for those searching for a boxing club for beginners or a Copenhagen boxing club that values inclusivity.
- **Flexible schedules:** Our boxing training schedule is designed to fit busy lives, with classes available in both Hellerup and Charlottenlund.
- **Challenge and growth:** From sparring classes to competition prep, every session is an opportunity to improve and inspire.

## Join the Movement—Celebrate Kvindernes Kampdag with AOBC in Copenhagen

This kvindernes kampdag, take the first step toward empowerment, fitness, and community. Whether you’re curious about fitness boxing, interested in youth boxing for your child, or eager to join a welcoming boxing community in Copenhagen, AOBC is here to help you grow stronger—physically and mentally.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?

AOBC offers beginner boxing classes tailored for all ages and fitness levels, including first boxing classes designed to teach fundamental techniques, boxing drills, and safety tips in a supportive environment.

### Can children join youth boxing programs at AOBC?

Yes, AOBC provides youth boxing and boxing training for kids programs focused on building confidence, discipline, and fitness through age-appropriate training sessions and expert coaching.

### Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?

AOBC has two convenient locations in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, both offering comprehensive boxing and fitness classes for all levels.

### Does AOBC provide sparring and competition training?

Absolutely. AOBC offers sparring classes and advanced boxing competition training programs for amateur boxers looking to improve their skills and prepare for competitive bouts.

### Who founded Ashira Oure Boxing Club and what is their expertise?

Ashira Oure Boxing Club was founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, who leads a team of experienced trainers dedicated to skill development, fitness, and personal coaching.
