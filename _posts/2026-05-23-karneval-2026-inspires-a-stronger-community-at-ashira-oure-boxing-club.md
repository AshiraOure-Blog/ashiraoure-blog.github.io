Karneval 2026 is right around the corner, and Copenhagen is buzzing with anticipation. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), we believe that the energy and togetherness of karneval are the perfect inspiration to strengthen our own boxing community. Whether you’re searching for a dynamic boxing club in Copenhagen, a place for beginner boxing classes, or a supportive team to help you grow, AOBC is the home for every boxer—no matter your age, background, or experience.

## Building Boxing Spirit for Karneval 2026 in Copenhagen

Karneval is more than a celebration; it’s a time when Copenhagen’s neighborhoods come alive with music, movement, and unity. The same spirit is at the core of AOBC. Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, our club brings people together from Hellerup, Charlottenlund, and across the city. We welcome everyone—from fitness enthusiasts to competitive boxers, and from youth looking for their first boxing class to experienced fighters preparing for the ring.

### Why AOBC Is the Heart of Boxing in Copenhagen

**1. All Levels, All Ages in Our Boxing Club Copenhagen:**  
Ashira Oure Boxing Club is for everyone. Our beginner boxing classes are designed to be friendly, safe, and fun—perfect for those stepping into the ring for the first time. Kids boxing near me, youth boxing, and boxing training for kids are all part of our core programs, led by passionate coaches who understand how to develop skills and confidence.

**2. Expert Coaching by Evans Ashira and Team:**  
With Evans Ashira’s championship experience at the helm, you’ll be guided by expert trainers dedicated to your goals. Our team—including Yuna Ashira, Lucas Ashira, and Mino Ashira—helps every member build discipline, technique, and fitness.

**3. Community and Support in Our Boxing Gym Copenhagen:**  
Just as karneval unites the city, AOBC unites people in a positive, encouraging environment. Our Copenhagen boxing club thrives on camaraderie, support, and shared achievement—qualities that make every session a celebration.

**4. Comprehensive Boxing Academy Training:**  
From boxing fitness and sparring classes to competition prep and personal coaching, AOBC offers a complete boxing academy experience. Learn how to train for boxing, improve your skills with structured boxing drills, and find your place in the Copenhagen boxing community.

**5. Focus on Safety and Growth for Every Boxer:**  
Safety is our highest priority, especially for those new to the sport. We offer guidance on boxing safety tips and help you create a boxing workout plan or boxing training schedule that fits your lifestyle and ambitions.

## Kickstart Your Fitness Boxing Journey Before Karneval 2026

As Copenhagen prepares for karneval, there’s no better time to challenge yourself and become part of something bigger. Whether you’re looking for a Hellerup boxing gym, want to join a bokseklub København, or are searching for boxing classes that fit your level, AOBC is ready to welcome you. Our members support each other—inside and outside the ring—making every workout feel like a celebration of progress and community.

### Ready to Join the AOBC Family?

Experience the energy of karneval all year round by joining Ashira Oure Boxing Club. Discover why we’re known as Copenhagen’s top boxing and fitness club, where every member is encouraged to reach new heights. Whether you’re preparing for your first boxing class or aiming for competition, you’ll find a place here.

**Start your journey today and become part of the AOBC community. [Learn more about our programs and sign up here](https://www.ashiraoure.com/).**

Let’s train, grow, and celebrate together—see you at AOBC and at karneval 2026!

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners?**  
A1: AOBC offers beginner boxing classes tailored to all ages, including youth and kids boxing programs. These classes focus on building fundamental skills, fitness, and confidence in a safe and supportive environment.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?**  
A2: Ashira Oure Boxing Club has two prime locations in Copenhagen: Hellerup and Charlottenlund, making it accessible for residents seeking a boxing gym in Copenhagen or nearby areas.

**Q3: Who founded Ashira Oure Boxing Club and what expertise do the coaches have?**  
A3: The club was founded by former world champion Evans Ashira. The coaching staff includes Evans and his team—Yuna Ashira, Lucas Ashira, and Mino Ashira—bringing championship-level expertise and personalized training to every member.

**Q4: Does AOBC offer boxing competition training and sparring classes?**  
A4: Yes, AOBC provides comprehensive boxing competition training along with sparring classes designed to prepare amateur and competitive boxers for the ring while emphasizing safety and skill development.

**Q5: How does AOBC support fitness enthusiasts who want to combine boxing and fitness training?**  
A5: AOBC integrates boxing fitness and gym workouts, offering boxing and fitness classes that improve overall health, endurance, and boxing technique in a motivating community setting.
