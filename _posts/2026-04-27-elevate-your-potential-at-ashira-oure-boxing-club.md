- **Sparring and Competition Training:** Ready to take it up a notch? AOBC offers structured sparring classes and boxing competition training for aspiring athletes and amateurs alike.
- **Youth and Kids Boxing:** AOBC is a boxing club for beginners of all ages. Our youth programs and kids boxing near me options nurture discipline, confidence, and healthy habits early on.
- **Personal Coaching:** Looking for tailored progress? Our coaches, inspired by the winning spirit of Evans Ashira, offer one-on-one sessions to help you crush your goals.
