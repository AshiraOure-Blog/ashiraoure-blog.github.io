**Why train at Ashira Oure Boxing Club in Copenhagen, regardless of the weather?**
- **Consistent Schedule:** Early morning or late evening, our classes run rain or shine.

- **Sparring and Competition Training:** For those chasing that next level, our sparring classes and boxing competition training will push your limits and build stamina.
- **Beginner and Youth Programs:** Searching for a boxing club for beginners or boxing training for kids near you? Our youth and kids boxing programs in Copenhagen are crafted to inspire confidence and discipline from an early age.
- **Personal Coaching:** Take your training to new heights with guidance from Evans Ashira and our elite coaching team.

**3. Can children join boxing training programs at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**
