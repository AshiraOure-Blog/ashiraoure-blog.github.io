At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, the excitement and unity found in Superligaen—the highest level of football in Denmark—echoes through every session in our Copenhagen boxing gyms. Just as Superligaen ignites passion and camaraderie among fans, AOBC channels that same energy into a supportive and inspiring environment for anyone who steps through our doors.

## The Heart of Boxing in Copenhagen

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, Ashira Oure Boxing Club stands out as Copenhagen’s premier destination for boxing, fitness, and personal development. With locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, AOBC is much more than a traditional boxing club. We are a thriving boxing community that welcomes everyone:  
- **Beginners discovering boxing for the first time**  
- **Fitness enthusiasts looking for a new challenge**  
- **Competitive fighters refining their craft**  
- **Young athletes joining our youth boxing programs**

Our expert-led classes, dynamic fitness workouts, structured sparring sessions, and personal coaching are designed to help every member push their limits—just like the footballers you see in Superligaen.

## All Levels, One Community at Copenhagen Boxing Club

At AOBC, we believe greatness comes from dedication, discipline, and a sense of belonging. Whether you’re searching for a **boxing club for beginners** or want advanced **boxing competition training**, our team—led by Evans Ashira—guides you through every jab, hook, and uppercut. We offer:

- **Boxing classes** for all levels, from first-timers to experienced boxers  
- **Fitness boxing** and conditioning sessions  
- **Sparring classes** with emphasis on technique and safety  
- **Boxing competition training** for those aiming to compete  
- **Youth boxing and kids boxing near me** programs, nurturing the next generation  
- **Personal coaching** tailored to individual goals

Our members motivate each other, celebrate progress, and uphold the spirit of fair play—values that mirror Superligaen’s celebrated sportsmanship.

## A Place to Grow Stronger in Hellerup Boxing Gym and Charlottenlund

Joining Ashira Oure Boxing Club is about more than learning to throw a punch. It’s about building self-confidence, improving your health, and finding your place in a vibrant Copenhagen boxing club community. From your first boxing class to your first sparring match, you’ll be supported by coaches and fellow members who want to see you succeed.

Our approach to boxing and fitness draws on the expertise of Evans Ashira, whose championship mindset infuses every aspect of AOBC. We emphasize skill development, a structured boxing training schedule, and the importance of discipline—qualities that help you thrive both inside and outside the ring.
