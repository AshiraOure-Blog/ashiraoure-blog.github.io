The excitement of the UFC inspires athletes and fitness enthusiasts around the globe to challenge themselves, develop discipline, and push the boundaries of their potential. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC)—Copenhagen’s premier boxing gym founded by legendary world champion Evans Ashira—those same values are brought to life every day in Hellerup and Charlottenlund. Whether you’re an aspiring athlete or just starting your fitness journey, AOBC welcomes you to a supportive, expert-led community built on skill, respect, and growth.

### Boxing Excellence for Every Level in Copenhagen

UFC fighters are known for their dedication, resilience, and versatility. At AOBC, we believe those qualities are for everyone—not just professionals in the ring or octagon. Our boxing club in Copenhagen offers:

- **Beginner boxing classes**: Step confidently into your first boxing class, guided by experienced coaches who prioritize safety, technique, and fun.  
- **Fitness boxing and workouts**: Enjoy dynamic sessions that combine cardio, strength, and boxing drills, perfect for those looking to build fitness in a motivating group setting.  
- **Sparring and competition prep**: For those with competitive ambitions, our structured sparring classes and boxing competition training provide a pathway to amateur boxing and beyond.  
- **Youth and kids boxing programs**: AOBC is proud to be a top destination for youth boxing and boxing training for kids in Copenhagen, creating a positive foundation for discipline, fitness, and self-confidence.

### Evans Ashira’s Legacy and AOBC’s Unique Approach to Boxing in Hellerup and Charlottenlund

At the heart of AOBC is Evans Ashira’s championship pedigree and passion for sharing the transformative power of boxing. Under his leadership, our team—including coaches like Yuna Ashira and Lucas Ashira—delivers personalized guidance, helping members of all ages and abilities reach their goals.

Our approach goes beyond punches and footwork:

- **Community atmosphere**: AOBC is more than a boxing gym Copenhagen can be proud of—it’s a place where friendships are forged and everyone is encouraged to grow, regardless of background or experience.
- **Discipline and wellness**: Emphasizing the holistic benefits of boxing, our programs foster mental focus, resilience, and overall wellness—qualities that echo the UFC spirit.
- **Expert coaching**: With personal coaching, tailored workout plans, and a deep commitment to safety, you’ll always feel supported on your journey.

### Why Choose Ashira Oure Boxing Club in Copenhagen?

With so many options for fitness and boxing in Copenhagen, AOBC stands out as a true community for all. Here’s what sets us apart:

- **All levels welcome**: Newcomers, fitness enthusiasts, and competitive boxers all find their place.
- **Flexible training**: From group boxing and fitness classes to personal coaching and sparring, your goals shape your path.
- **Trusted expertise**: Evans Ashira’s experience as a world champion and the trusted AOBC coaching team ensure safe, effective, and inspiring training.
- **Central locations**: Conveniently situated in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, AOBC is accessible for boxing enthusiasts across Copenhagen.
