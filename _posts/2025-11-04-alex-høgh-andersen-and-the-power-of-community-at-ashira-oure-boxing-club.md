## Why Ashira Oure Boxing Club Is Copenhagen’s Top Choice for Boxing and Fitness

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC stands as a beacon of excellence for boxing in Copenhagen. With state-of-the-art facilities in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, our boxing gym welcomes all—from absolute beginners to seasoned fighters. Guided by expert trainers, including Evans Ashira himself, every member receives the support and challenge they need to reach their personal best.

### What Sets AOBC Apart as a Leading Boxing Gym Copenhagen

- **Expert-Led Classes:** Learn from world-class coaches in beginner boxing classes, advanced sparring sessions, and competition training.
- **Inclusive Community:** AOBC is a boxing club Copenhagen locals trust, where everyone belongs. Our members range from teens taking their first boxing class to adults refining their skills.
- **Youth and Kids Programs:** Searching for *kids boxing near me*? Our youth boxing and boxing training for kids programs instill discipline, confidence, and fitness in a fun and safe setting.
- **Personal Coaching:** For those seeking tailored guidance, our personal coaching delivers one-on-one attention and a personalized boxing workout plan.
- **Fitness Focus:** Not just a boxing academy, AOBC also offers fitness boxing and boxing and fitness classes—perfect for anyone wanting to combine boxing skill with total-body conditioning.

## Inspired by Champions, Driven by Community at AOBC Copenhagen

Like Alex Høgh Andersen’s rise to fame, progress at AOBC is built on perseverance and support. Our members motivate each other, forging friendships that extend beyond the gym. Whether you want to master boxing drills, understand boxing sparring rules, or join a gym with boxing at its heart, AOBC is your home for growth.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed to teach fundamental boxing skills, fitness boxing, and safe sparring techniques. These classes cater to all ages and skill levels, making it ideal for those new to boxing.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?**  
AOBC operates two main gyms located in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, providing accessible boxing training options across Copenhagen.

**Q3: Does AOBC provide youth and kids boxing training programs?**  
Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth and kids boxing programs that focus on fitness, discipline, and confidence in a safe and supportive environment.

**Q4: Can I get personal coaching and competition training at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**  
Absolutely. AOBC provides personal coaching and boxing competition training tailored to help amateur boxers and competitive athletes improve their skills and prepare for bouts.

**Q5: How does Ashira Oure Boxing Club support fitness enthusiasts in Copenhagen?**  
Beyond boxing skills, AOBC incorporates fitness boxing and boxing and fitness classes designed to enhance overall conditioning, endurance, and strength for all fitness levels.
