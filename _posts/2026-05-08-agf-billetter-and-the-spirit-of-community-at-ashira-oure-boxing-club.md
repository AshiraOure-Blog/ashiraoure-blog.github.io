## Why Ashira Oure Boxing Club is Copenhagen’s Premier Training Ground

Founded by world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC has become the boxing club Copenhagen trusts for skill development, fitness, and discipline. With locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, our club is a welcoming space for everyone—from complete beginners to competitive athletes.

Here’s what sets AOBC apart:

- **Expert-Led Classes:** Train under the guidance of experienced coaches, including Evans Ashira himself. Our trainers are passionate about helping you reach your potential, whether you’re learning your first jab or preparing for a tournament.
- **All Levels Welcome:** New to boxing? Our beginner boxing classes and boxing club for beginners are designed to help you start strong, safely, and confidently. Looking to take your skills further? Join our sparring classes or competition prep sessions.
- **Youth and Kids Programs:** AOBC is a leading choice for youth boxing and boxing training for kids in Copenhagen. We believe in using boxing to build confidence, discipline, and fitness from a young age—making us a go-to for families searching for “kids boxing near me.”
- **Fitness Focus:** Beyond technique, our fitness boxing and boxing workout plans cater to those looking to boost overall health, lose weight, and improve endurance. Whether you’re interested in boxing and fitness classes or personal coaching, there’s a program for every goal.
- **Supportive Community:** AOBC’s members and trainers form a close-knit boxing community, where everyone is encouraged to support, inspire, and challenge one another. It’s more than a gym—it’s a place where lifelong friendships and champions are made.

## Embracing the Energy of Copenhagen’s Sports Culture

Just as securing agf billetter connects fans across Denmark, joining Ashira Oure Boxing Club brings you into a thriving network of athletes, families, and fitness enthusiasts. Our members come from all over Copenhagen, drawn together by a shared passion for boxing and well-being. At AOBC, you’ll find:

- A focus on **discipline** and **personal growth**
- Expert advice on **how to train for boxing** and crafting your own **boxing training schedule**
- Guidance on **boxing safety tips** and preparing for your **first boxing class**
- A safe, welcoming space for women, youth, and adults alike

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed for all skill levels, focusing on safe training, foundational boxing techniques, and building confidence. These classes are perfect for those new to boxing in Copenhagen, including youth and adults.

**Q2: Where are the Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?**  
A2: Ashira Oure Boxing Club has two primary locations in Copenhagen: Hellerup and Charlottenlund. Both gyms provide comprehensive boxing training, fitness sessions, and community events.

**Q3: Does AOBC provide youth and kids boxing programs?**  
A3: Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth boxing and boxing training for kids programs aimed at developing discipline, fitness, and confidence in a safe and supportive environment.

**Q4: What types of boxing training does AOBC offer for competitive boxers?**  
A4: AOBC provides sparring classes, competition preparation training, and personalized coaching to help competitive boxers enhance their skills and perform at their best in tournaments.

**Q5: How can I start my first boxing class at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**  
A5: You can book your first class through the AOBC website. The club welcomes beginners with tailored sessions and expert guidance to ensure a positive and safe introduction to boxing.
