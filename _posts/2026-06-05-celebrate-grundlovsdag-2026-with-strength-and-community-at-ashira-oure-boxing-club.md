## A Boxing Club for All in Copenhagen

Ashira Oure Boxing Club stands as a beacon for boxers and fitness enthusiasts across Copenhagen. Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC is more than just a boxing gym—it’s a community where beginners, youth, and seasoned competitors come together to pursue skill, discipline, and wellness.

### What Makes AOBC Copenhagen’s Premier Boxing Club?

- **Expert-Led Classes for All Levels:**  
  From beginner boxing classes to advanced sparring, every session at AOBC is guided by experienced coaches passionate about helping you improve—no matter your starting point.

- **Fitness and Boxing Combined:**  
  Looking for a gym with boxing that boosts both your stamina and your confidence? AOBC’s fitness boxing and boxing and fitness classes are designed to challenge your body and refresh your mind.

- **Youth and Kids Programs:**  
  AOBC is committed to nurturing the next generation of champions. Our youth boxing and boxing training for kids blend fun, safety, and real skill-building in an encouraging environment. If you’re searching for “kids boxing near me” or a youth boxing academy in Copenhagen, AOBC is your answer.

- **Sparring and Competition Training:**  
  Ready to test your skills? Our sparring classes and boxing competition training are open to all who want to push their limits—always with safety, discipline, and respect.

- **Supportive Community:**  
  The heartbeat of AOBC is its people. Whether you’re in Hellerup or Charlottenlund, you’ll find a welcoming community that celebrates every win, big or small.

## Why Start Boxing on Grundlovsdag 2026?

Grundlovsdag is about coming together and building something lasting. Boxing at AOBC is much the same:  
- **Build Strength and Confidence:** Every punch and every drill help you discover your true potential.  
- **Foster Discipline:** The routines you learn in the ring translate to resilience and focus outside of it.  
- **Join a Lasting Community:** From your first boxing class to your hundredth, AOBC’s members and coaches are with you every step of the way.

If you’ve always wondered how to train for boxing, or you’re ready to take your fitness to the next level, Grundlovsdag 2026 is the perfect moment to begin.

**Q5: How can boxing at AOBC improve overall fitness and wellness?**
