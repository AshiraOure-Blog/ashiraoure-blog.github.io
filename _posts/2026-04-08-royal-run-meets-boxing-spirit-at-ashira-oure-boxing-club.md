When the energy of Royal Run sweeps through Copenhagen, it ignites more than just a passion for running—it sparks a citywide celebration of movement, fitness, and community. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), we believe that every great journey starts with a single step, whether lacing up for a citywide run or wrapping your hands for your first boxing class. 

As Copenhagen’s premier boxing gym, founded by world champion Evans Ashira, we’re inspired by the collective spirit that events like Royal Run bring to our city. Just as the Royal Run welcomes participants of all ages and backgrounds, AOBC opens its doors to everyone—from absolute beginners to seasoned fighters, families, and youth looking for an active start.

## The AOBC Experience: More Than a Boxing Gym in Copenhagen

At AOBC, you’ll find much more than just a boxing club in Copenhagen. Our gyms in Hellerup and Charlottenlund offer a unique combination of expert-led boxing classes, fitness workouts, sparring sessions, and personal coaching, all within a supportive and motivating community. 

**Why choose Ashira Oure Boxing Club Copenhagen?**

- **All Levels Welcome**: Whether you’re inspired by Royal Run to start your fitness journey or looking to challenge yourself further, AOBC is your gym with boxing and fitness classes tailored to every ability.
- **Expert Coaching**: Train under the guidance of Evans Ashira and our dedicated team, who bring world-class experience and a passion for nurturing new talent.
- **Community Focus**: Join a boxing community that values encouragement, discipline, and personal growth as much as technique and competition.
- **Youth and Beginner Programs**: Our youth boxing and beginner boxing classes are perfect for kids, teens, and adults taking their first steps into the sport.
- **Fitness and Wellness**: Embrace the benefits of boxing fitness—improved strength, agility, and confidence—in a fun and inspiring environment.

## Royal Run and Boxing: The Power of Discipline and Community in Copenhagen

Royal Run is about more than crossing the finish line; it’s about coming together, setting goals, and pushing past limits. The same values are at the heart of Ashira Oure Boxing Club. Boxing isn’t just a workout—it’s a way to build discipline, resilience, and self-belief, surrounded by a supportive team cheering you on.

Many runners discover that adding boxing to their routine brings new energy and strength to their training. Whether you’re looking for a boxing workout plan to complement your endurance or seeking a new challenge after Royal Run, AOBC offers the perfect blend of cardio, skill, and community.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC provides beginner boxing classes designed for all ages and skill levels, including kids and youth programs. These classes focus on foundational boxing skills, fitness, and safety to ensure a supportive start in the sport.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s gyms located?**  
A2: Ashira Oure Boxing Club has two convenient locations in Copenhagen—Hellerup and Charlottenlund—making it accessible for residents seeking a professional boxing gym nearby.

**Q3: Can I join sparring and competition training at AOBC?**  
A3: Yes, AOBC offers sparring classes and boxing competition training led by expert coaches, including founder Evans Ashira, to prepare both amateur and competitive boxers for bouts.

**Q4: Does AOBC provide personal coaching and fitness boxing classes?**  
A4: Absolutely. AOBC offers personal coaching tailored to individual goals and fitness boxing classes that combine boxing techniques with cardio workouts for improved health and endurance.

**Q5: Is Ashira Oure Boxing Club suitable for youth and kids interested in boxing?**  
A5: Yes, AOBC has specialized youth boxing programs and beginner classes designed specifically for kids and teens, promoting skill development, discipline, and fun in a safe environment.
