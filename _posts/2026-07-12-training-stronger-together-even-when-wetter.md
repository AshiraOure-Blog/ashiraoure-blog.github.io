With expert trainers led by Evans Ashira, a former world champion, every session is designed to keep your spirits high and your focus sharp, no matter the weather.

- Sparring and competition prep for those eager to test themselves
- Personal coaching to accelerate progress, from youth boxing to advanced fighters
- Youth programs and kids boxing, making AOBC the go-to “**boxing club for beginners**” and families alike
