Every athlete’s journey has its share of setbacks. In the world of boxing, “ulykke”—the Danish word for accident or misfortune—reminds us that challenges are inevitable. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), we believe that how you respond to setbacks is what truly shapes you, both in the ring and beyond.

#### Rising Above Ulykke at Copenhagen’s Premier Boxing Gym

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC is more than just a boxing gym in Copenhagen. With locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, our club is a community where everyone—from complete beginners to seasoned fighters—finds support, motivation, and inspiration. Whether you’re taking your first boxing class, preparing for competition, or helping your child discover the joys of youth boxing, AOBC welcomes you with open arms.

#### Safety, Skill, and Support—Our Commitment to You

At AOBC, we know that boxing is as much about discipline and skill as it is about fitness and fun. We take every precaution to ensure your safety, offering:

- **Expert-led classes** for all levels, from beginner boxing classes to advanced sparring sessions  
- Strict adherence to **boxing safety tips** and sparring rules  
- Personalized coaching to help you grow at your own pace  
- A supportive environment where every member is encouraged to overcome obstacles

Even when ulykke strikes—whether it’s a minor setback in training or a tough day in the ring—our coaches and community will stand by you, helping you rebuild confidence and skill.

#### Building Strength Through Community

Ashira Oure Boxing Club is built on the belief that everyone deserves a chance to become stronger—physically and mentally. Our programs include:

- **Boxing and fitness classes** for all ages  
- **Youth boxing** and **kids boxing near me** options for families in Copenhagen  
- **Sparring classes** and **boxing competition training** for those aiming higher  
- Guidance on **how to train for boxing**, including boxing drills and workout plans tailored to your goals

With AOBC, you are never alone. Our community stands together, celebrating every victory and supporting each other through every ulykke.

#### Why Choose AOBC for Your Boxing Journey?

- **Expertise**: Train with Evans Ashira and a team of experienced coaches  
- **Community**: Join a welcoming copenhagen boxing club where everyone matters  
- **Discipline**: Learn the values that define true athletes  
- **Growth**: From your first boxing class to your first competition, we’re with you at every step

#### Ready to Start Stronger Than Ever?

Don’t let ulykke hold you back from achieving your goals. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club, every setback is an opportunity to come back stronger. Whether you’re searching for a beginner-friendly bokseklub københavn or a top-tier boxing academy, you’ll find your place here.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC offers beginner-friendly boxing classes designed for all ages and fitness levels. Our expert coaches provide personalized training to help beginners learn boxing fundamentals safely and effectively.

**Q2: Where are Ashira Oure Boxing Club’s locations in Copenhagen?**  
A2: Ashira Oure Boxing Club has two convenient locations in Copenhagen: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, making it easy for members across the city to access top-tier boxing training.

**Q3: Does AOBC provide youth and kids boxing programs?**  
A3: Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth boxing and kids boxing classes designed to teach discipline, fitness, and boxing skills in a supportive environment for young athletes.

**Q4: How does Ashira Oure Boxing Club ensure safety during sparring and training?**  
A4: AOBC strictly follows boxing safety tips and sparring rules, led by experienced coaches who prioritize member safety through proper technique, protective gear, and supervised training sessions.

**Q5: Can I prepare for boxing competitions at Ashira Oure Boxing Club?**  
A5: Absolutely. AOBC provides comprehensive boxing competition training and sparring classes tailored for amateur boxers aiming to compete locally and nationally.
