## AOBC: Where Champions and Beginners Train Side by Side in Copenhagen

Founded by former world champion Evans Ashira, AOBC is more than just a boxing club in Copenhagen. Whether you’re searching for your first “boxing class” or aiming for the podium in a “boxing competition,” AOBC’s doors are open. Our locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund are designed to foster growth, discipline, and fitness for all ages and skill levels.

### What Makes AOBC Special as a Boxing Club in Copenhagen?

- **Expert-Led Classes:** Learn from top trainers, including Evans Ashira himself, with years of international experience.
- **Inclusive Community:** AOBC is a boxing community where everyone—from youth boxers to adults—is encouraged to support and motivate each other.
- **Diverse Programs:** From “beginner boxing classes” to advanced sparring, youth programs, and personal coaching, there’s something for everyone.
- **Focus on Fitness:** Our approach blends “boxing fitness” and “fitness boxing” to ensure you get stronger, faster, and healthier—no matter your starting point.
- **Safety and Discipline:** We emphasize “boxing safety tips” and technical skill to help every member train with confidence.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?
AOBC offers beginner boxing classes tailored to help newcomers develop fundamental skills, improve fitness, and build confidence in a supportive environment at both Hellerup and Charlottenlund locations.

### Can kids and youth join boxing training at AOBC?
Yes, AOBC provides specialized youth boxing and boxing training for kids programs led by experienced coaches, focusing on skill development, fitness, and discipline in a safe and encouraging setting.

### How does Ashira Oure Boxing Club prepare fighters for competitions?
The club offers structured boxing competition training and personalized boxing workout plans designed to enhance technique, endurance, and sparring skills, preparing members for amateur and professional bouts.

### Where is Ashira Oure Boxing Club located in Copenhagen?
AOBC has two convenient locations in Hellerup and Charlottenlund, serving the Copenhagen area and providing top-quality boxing and fitness services to the community.

### Who is Evans Ashira and what is his role at AOBC?
Evans Ashira is a former world boxing champion and the founder of Ashira Oure Boxing Club. He leads expert training sessions and ensures AOBC maintains high standards in skill, fitness, and discipline.
