When Copenhagen weather brings rain, wind, or sunshine, the energy inside Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC) never falters. Whether you’re a newcomer searching for your first boxing class or an experienced fighter preparing for competition, AOBC is your home for boxing in every season. Founded by world champion Evans Ashira, our club in Hellerup and Charlottenlund is more than just a boxing gym—it’s a vibrant community where discipline, skill, and fitness flourish, no matter what the forecast holds.

## Training That Rises Above the Weather in Copenhagen

In Denmark, the weather can be unpredictable. Some days invite you outside; others make you want to stay in. At AOBC, we believe that true champions train regardless of the elements. Our modern, welcoming facilities in Copenhagen’s Hellerup and Charlottenlund create the perfect indoor environment to pursue your boxing and fitness goals year-round.

Here’s how AOBC helps you stay motivated and unstoppable, rain or shine:

- **Beginner Boxing Classes in Copenhagen:** Never boxed before? Our expert coaches guide you through your first boxing class, making sure you feel confident and empowered.
- **Fitness Boxing Workouts:** Boost your health and wellness with high-energy fitness boxing classes that keep your motivation high, even when it’s gloomy outside.
- **Sparring and Competition Training:** For those aiming higher, our sparring classes and boxing competition training programs are designed by Evans Ashira himself, helping you develop advanced skills and discipline.
- **Youth and Kids Boxing Programs:** AOBC’s youth boxing programs give kids and teens a positive outlet, teaching resilience and teamwork in a safe, supportive environment.
- **Personal Coaching:** Individualized plans ensure that every member, from beginners to competitive boxers, receives personal attention to reach their goals.

## A Copenhagen Boxing Community That Grows Stronger Together

The Copenhagen boxing community is at the heart of AOBC. Our members support each other through every training session, regardless of the season. The camaraderie is evident—from the first warm-up drill to the final round on the bags. Here, athletes of all levels come together, inspired by Evans Ashira’s passion and commitment.

AOBC’s doors are open to everyone:

- **Beginners** eager to get active and try something new with boxing classes for beginners
- **Fitness enthusiasts** looking for a dynamic gym with boxing and fitness classes
- **Competitive amateurs and aspiring champions** pursuing boxing in Copenhagen and preparing for competitions
- **Youth and families** seeking programs like kids boxing near me or youth boxing in Copenhagen
