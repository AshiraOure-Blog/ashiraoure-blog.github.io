In Copenhagen, determination and community spirit go hand in hand—qualities embodied by Alexandra Bøje, whose athletic journey continues to inspire both newcomers and seasoned athletes. At Ashira Oure Boxing Club (AOBC), founded by the renowned Evans Ashira, these same values are the heartbeat of our boxing club community in Hellerup and Charlottenlund.

#### Championing Inclusivity from the First Punch

Whether you’re lacing up your gloves for your **first boxing class** or preparing for competition, AOBC welcomes everyone. Our doors are open to absolute beginners, ambitious youth, fitness enthusiasts, and competitive boxers. With a range of classes including **beginner boxing classes**, **youth boxing**, **sparring classes**, and **boxing competition training**, there’s a perfect fit for every goal and skill level.

Our expert coaches—led by Evans Ashira himself—bring out the best in every member, focusing not only on technique but also on personal growth, discipline, and confidence. The supportive environment at AOBC makes it easy for anyone to start their journey, just like Alexandra Bøje once did in her sporting career.

#### Building Skills, Fitness, and Lasting Friendships

Boxing at AOBC is about more than just punches and footwork. It’s about building a strong foundation in fitness, resilience, and camaraderie. Our **boxing classes** combine skill-based drills with dynamic workouts—ideal for those seeking **boxing fitness**, **fitness boxing**, or simply a more engaging way to stay fit.

Here’s what you’ll find at AOBC:

- **Youth and kids boxing** programs designed to foster confidence and discipline in a fun, secure setting.
- **Personal coaching** sessions tailored to individual needs, whether you’re aiming for competition or pursuing a healthier lifestyle.
- **Sparring classes** and competitive training, always guided by safety and respect for every participant.
- A welcoming community where everyone, from beginners to champions, supports each other’s progress.

#### Your Journey Starts Here

Inspired by local athletes like Alexandra Bøje, AOBC is committed to nurturing talent and passion for boxing in Copenhagen. Our members come from all walks of life, united by a shared drive for self-improvement and mutual support. Whether you’re searching for a **boxing club for beginners**, a **boxing academy**, or the best **boxing gym Copenhagen** has to offer, AOBC is your destination.

Ready to start your own journey, build your fitness, and join a vibrant community? Visit [Ashira Oure Boxing Club](https://www.ashiraoure.com/) to find out more about our classes, programs, and how you can become part of Copenhagen’s leading boxing family.

Let Alexandra Bøje’s example inspire you to step into the ring—at AOBC, every champion has a beginning, and every beginning is celebrated.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1: What types of boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer for beginners in Copenhagen?**  
A1: AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed to introduce new members to fundamental techniques, fitness training, and boxing safety. These classes are perfect for anyone starting their boxing journey in Copenhagen.

**Q2: Where are the Ashira Oure Boxing Club locations in Copenhagen?**  
A2: AOBC has two main locations: one in Hellerup and another in Charlottenlund, both easily accessible for residents in Copenhagen and surrounding areas.

**Q3: Does Ashira Oure Boxing Club provide training programs for youth and kids?**  
A3: Yes, AOBC offers specialized youth and kids boxing programs focused on building confidence, discipline, and fitness in a safe and fun environment.

**Q4: How does Ashira Oure Boxing Club support competitive boxers in Copenhagen?**  
A4: AOBC provides sparring classes, boxing competition training, and personal coaching to help competitive boxers prepare effectively for matches and tournaments.

**Q5: Can fitness enthusiasts join Ashira Oure Boxing Club for boxing and fitness workouts?**  
A5: Absolutely. AOBC combines boxing drills with fitness boxing workouts, making it an excellent gym for those looking to improve their overall fitness through boxing.
