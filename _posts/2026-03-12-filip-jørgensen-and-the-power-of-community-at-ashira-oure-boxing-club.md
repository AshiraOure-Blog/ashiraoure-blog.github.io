## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What beginner boxing classes does Ashira Oure Boxing Club offer in Copenhagen?  
AOBC offers beginner boxing classes designed for all skill levels, especially for those new to boxing. These classes focus on fundamentals, fitness boxing, and developing proper techniques in a supportive environment at their Hellerup and Charlottenlund locations.

### Can children and youth join boxing programs at AOBC?  
Yes, AOBC provides specialized youth boxing and boxing training for kids programs. These classes help build confidence, discipline, and athleticism in a safe and encouraging community setting in Copenhagen.

### Who founded Ashira Oure Boxing Club and what is his background?  
Ashira Oure Boxing Club was founded by former world champion boxer Evans Ashira. His expertise and personal coaching ensure all members receive world-class training tailored to their goals, whether fitness, sparring, or competition prep.

### What types of sparring and competition training are available at AOBC?  
AOBC offers sparring classes and boxing competition training designed for amateur and aspiring competitive boxers. Their programs emphasize skill development, boxing sparring rules, and safety tips to prepare members effectively for the ring.

### How does AOBC support fitness enthusiasts looking for boxing workouts?  
AOBC combines boxing and fitness classes to deliver comprehensive boxing workouts and fitness boxing routines. This approach benefits beginners and experienced athletes seeking to improve endurance, strength, and boxing skills within a motivating community.
